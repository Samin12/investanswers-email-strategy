## 1. TABI — "Top And Bottom Indicator" (Bitcoin)

**What it is:** A composite Bitcoin cycle-timing model sold as a TradingView indicator ("IA Bitcoin Top & Bottom"). Built over ~2 years after James missed the 2021 top (he had projected $89K). Launched ~Nov 2023.

**Methodology:** A mathematical model (explicitly "not chart TA") that normalizes **15–19 on-chain + market metrics** into a single confidence score rendered as a color-coded heatmap:
- *Valuation:* price-to-350DMA ratio, NVTS, Pi Cycle ratio, Puell Multiple
- *Network health:* BTC dominance, hash rate, transaction counts, retail holder counts
- *Behavior:* long-term-holder (hodler) activity, miner flows/reserves, whale flows, % supply in profit, perpetual futures ratio

**Signal ladder:** dark blue = "buy as much as you can" → green = good buy → yellow = slow buying → light orange = layer out → dark orange = arm sell triggers → red = exit. Key thresholds: Pi Cycle +50% = danger / −50% = max accumulation; 99% of supply in profit = top red flag. His stated doctrine: "Fundamental analysis must come first… then use TA to time entry/exit." Known blind spot (his words): can't see exogenous catalysts (e.g., ETF approvals).

**Pricing/current:** $24/mo (cut from $50), requires TradingView. Context for current readings: BTC topped ~$126K (late 2025), entered a 40% drawdown by Feb 5, 2026 (intraday $72.5K; he called ~$70K the "historic floor"), and broke below $66K on June 3, 2026 — his June 9, 2026 newsletter "HISTORIC SHAKEOUT" teases a "crazy new Bottom Signal."

## 5. The Weekly Edge / #TAlpha + IADSS mean-reversion system

**What it is:** "**TA Summary: The Weekly Edge – #TAlpha**" — a weekly Patreon technical-analysis recap (TAlpha = "Technical Alpha"). Recent editions: *Apr 6–10, 2026 "Risk Happens Fast"*, *May 25–29, 2026 "Everything Extended!!!"*, *"TA Weekly Edge Recap — Bumpy Week" (May 15, 2026)*, *"#TAlpha (3359): Marvelling at Marvell."*

**Underlying engine — IADSS (InvestAnswers Decision Support System):** four paid TradingView components — **Trend** (two fast MAs), **Optimized Trend** (orange = stay out / blue-gold = trend turn), **Mean Reversion** ("MR" oscillator: green dot below band = buy turning up; red dot above = sell turning down), **Confluence Model** (enhanced Bollinger-band buy/sell arrows with bearish→bullish bias setting). Optimized for the **4-hour chart** ("my magic timeframe"); zoom-out sequence = daily → 4H → 15m/5m to strike. Claimed MR backtest: **90.7% win rate**; "confluence math" = stacking 81% + 83% + 88% win-rate models → ~97–99% confidence. Sequence: MR fires first ("canary"), confluence confirms, trend turn = final gate. Entry style: "double-tap" 30% + 30% buys; LIFO 30% trims on exits. Related tool: **PTOS** (price-trend oscillator / "inverse pair trading on steroids," e.g. TSLL/TSLQ, PLTR/PLTD pairs, used to time directional options). Critics (The CryptoG) allege the indicators repaint and backtests are unreliable; Executive-tier access ≈ $200/mo. The 200DMA features in his macro commentary, but the MR system itself keys off proprietary bands, not the 200DMA specifically.

## 7. Synthetic longs on TSLA, deep-ITM LEAPS, covered-call hedging

**The synthetic long structure (the "$30 to make $270" idea):** sell a long-dated put + buy a long-dated call for a tiny net debit. Documented example (broken down publicly by G_Dinero, who teaches IA's trades): **Dec 17, 2027 TSLA 350/330 "custom synthetic long"** — sold the 350 put for $115 ($11,500 credit), bought the 330 call for $148 ($14,800), **net debit ≈ $33/share (~$3,300)**, breakeven $363; entry after TSLA wicked to ~$345.50 on tariff news; collateral via margin against his "big bag" of stock. At TSLA $1,000 the call is worth $670 → ~$63,700 profit = **20.3x**; the "$30 to make $270" framing is the same structure quoted at a ~$600 TSLA outcome (~9x). Other documented variants: Jan 2027 TSLA 380 SL; Jun 17 2027 TSLA 230 SL. He's run synthetics since at least 2021–22 (e.g., META synthetic long at ~$4/share net when META P/E hit 7, Oct 2022 — cited in "Best Trades of 2023").

**Deep-ITM LEAPS doctrine (Apr 19, 2026 video, verbatim logic):** LEAPS = "stock replacement with built-in leverage," bought only at "extreme bottoms" (e.g., TSLA $330, NVDA $88 dip-day in Q1 2025 — "I dipped twice that day"); never swing-traded; exit only if thesis changes; accept 60% option drawdowns on 30% stock dips. First TSLA LEAPS in 2017. Core loop: **"Build a bag, build a mattress"** — stock/LEAPS bag becomes margin collateral → sell covered calls at MR tops (1–3%/mo, or 5–6% waiting for "big fat pitches") and sell puts at MR bottoms ("sell puts to fund calls… the money's in the selling, not the buying"; 30–45 DTE).

**STM/ALAB-style hedging:** Patreon runs dedicated trade posts — ["EXCLUSIVE VIDEO — Trade Breakdown: ALAB"](https://www.patreon.com/posts/exclusive-video-150517367), ["How to Take Profits, Hedge and use Covered Calls"](https://www.patreon.com/posts/exclusive-video-143947973), and **Rule #111 (June 4, 2026) doubles as a "Heads up on ALAB Covered Call trade."** Pattern: after a parabolic run in an IA13 name (MRVL, ALAB), he sells covered calls against it as the hedge/income leg. (Laura "Options Goddess" is the community's advanced-options instructor; her 301 course = Synthetic Long / Modified Synthetic Long.)
