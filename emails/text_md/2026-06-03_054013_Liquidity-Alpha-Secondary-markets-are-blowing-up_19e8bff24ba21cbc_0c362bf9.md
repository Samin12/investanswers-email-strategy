 You can now sell shares in private companies at a premium to their net asset value (NAV). This is a huge shift — private stock is no longer just illiquid paper. This is a game changer. 

 The main thing people are talking about here is pre ipo allocations... and they are massive. $250M deals, $1BN deals, $5BN deals... the pre ipo ie secondary market is red hot. People are clamoring to buy at any price. 

 Billions are traded now on SpaceX, Anthropic, Anduril etc. 

 I spent a good chunk of this evening talking w CEO of Forge Global.. they have the most amazing plans incoming.
