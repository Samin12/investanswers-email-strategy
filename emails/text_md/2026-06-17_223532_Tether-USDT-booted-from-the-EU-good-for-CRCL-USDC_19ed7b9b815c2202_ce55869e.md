 TLDR Regulation just handed Circle a bigger slice of Europe's on-ramp and trading volume. More USDC in circulation = direct, recurring revenue growth with almost no extra cost.

 Details EU regulators just forced the world's biggest stablecoin (Tether/USDT, $186B supply) off major licensed European exchanges because it skipped MiCA compliance rules. Circle's USDC ($75B supply) is fully compliant and now one of the main options left on those platforms.

 Why Circle Stock and Solana (where it issues) should benefit. 

 Circle earns almost all its revenue from interest on the cash backing USDC (Q1 2026 reserve income: $653M, driven by circulation growth). Even a modest EU shift (a few billion dollars moving into USDC) adds meaningful extra reserves Б├▓ higher interest income at current yields.
