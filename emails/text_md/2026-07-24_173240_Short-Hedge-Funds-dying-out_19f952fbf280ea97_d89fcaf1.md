 Not sure if this is an AI Impact or the fact that going long term "short-only" is a deadly strategy, but short only funds are a dying breed. There are only 5 left. 

 Note this week has been a great time to be short many names. I am only hedge ALAB currently. 

 Short Funds that were wiped out recently Melvin Capital (Gabe Plotkin) — Heavily short GameStop 

 White Square Capital (Florian Kronawitter) — London-based fund that shorted GameStop

 Jim Chanos / Kynikos Associates (later Chanos & Co.) — ran a multi-year short against Tesla that cost the fund badly as the stock rose thousands of percent. 

 Michael Burry / Scion Asset Management — He has run various Tesla, Nvidia, etc shorts and does not believe in AI - thinks its a bubble. Wiped out in Nov 2025. 

 Light Street Capital (Glen Kacher)
