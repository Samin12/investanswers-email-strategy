 Jacob breaks down the AMD Trade Alert from today: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=od7ekiflNkI 

 James sold covered calls on AMD (strike $420, premium $40) as a hedge to lock in some profit and protect big recent gains if the stock dropped.

 AMD rocketed past $460 fast. The calls he sold are now worth more, so if he waited to close them later it could cost him a lot (e.g. $80 loss if AMD hits $500). He was clearly nervous so the minute he could unwind at least half the trade - he did. 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Today he closed half at just $42 — a tiny $2 loss — while the stock dipped a bit. He’s getting out early because:

 He wants to keep riding the upside if AMD keeps running

 He’s raising cash right now

 He knew market was oversold on Oil and CPI BS

 Bottom line : Covered calls worked as a mild hedge, but in a strong uptrend they can cap your gains. He’s trimming the hedge now so he doesn’t get stuck while AMD potentially goes higher. Mean reversion is coming eventually — just not today.
