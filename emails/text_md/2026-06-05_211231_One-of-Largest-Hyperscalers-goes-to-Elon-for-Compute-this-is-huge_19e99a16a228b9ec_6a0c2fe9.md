 Google just signed a massive deal to buy AI compute from SpaceX.

 Google will pay SpaceX $920 million every month through June 2029 (nearly $30 billion total) for access to a huge cluster of ~110,000 NVIDIA GPUs plus the supporting CPUs and memory.

 This comes right after SpaceX’s similar big deal with Anthropic.

 Why this is actually HUGE news: One of the largest hyperscalers on the planet (Google) is going cap in hand to Elon Musk’s SpaceX because they need serious AI computing power that badly.

 Even with their own enormous data centers and custom chips (TPUs) Google is locking in this capacity for years at nearly a billion dollars a month. That tells you how tight the AI compute shortage really is right now — the biggest players are outsourcing big chunks of it. This is in line w my note on Monday... how NOBODY HAS COMPUTE IN 2026 and NOBODY HAS COMPUTE in 2027

 For SpaceX, this is a monster win:

 Massive, high-margin recurring revenue

 Major validation of their Colossus-class GPU clusters

 Perfect timing right before their IPO

 The AI infrastructure boom just got another very loud signal. Even Google is turning to Uncle Elon for the GPUs.
