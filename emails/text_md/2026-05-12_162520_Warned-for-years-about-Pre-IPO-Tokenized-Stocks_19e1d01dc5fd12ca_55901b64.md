 Crypto Remains the Wild West and I warned for years. You've always heard me say "PURE FORM ALWAYS" 

 These tokens usually aren’t real shares. They’re synthetic IOUs made by random third parties who never got permission from the actual company.

 Proof just dropped today (May 12, 2026):

 Anthropic PreStocks on Solana crashed 45% in 24 hours. The “market cap” went from $1.4 trillion → $762 billion. Roughly $638 billion in paper value vanished overnight.

 “Unauthorized stock sales or transfers are void and will not be recognized.”

 Those Pre IPO tokens ? They’re fake. Nobody there to honor them. You own nothing.

 Boom. Price discovery happened the hard way.

 No legal backing — The company can (and just did) say “we don’t know you.”

 Zero regulation — No SEC-style protection, no real ownership rights.

 Hype-driven vapor — Prices can be anything until the company or regulators step in… then it collapses.

 Counterparty risk — The token issuer can rug, disappear, or get sued — and you’re left holding worthless code.

 This isn’t investing. It’s gambling on someone else’s unauthorized bet that the real company will never find out or care.
