 Tesla’s Semi momentum is surging, with publicly reported orders now exceeding 920 units—a total valuation estimated at well over $230 million 🔥

 Based on the latest public data, here is the breakdown of confirmed buyers and their fleet commitments:

 * WattEV: 370 units

 * UPS: 125 units

 * Walmart: ~130 units

 * PepsiCo: 100 units

 * Sysco: 50 units

 * Anheuser-Busch: 40 units

 * Big F Transport: 40 units

 * Loblaw: 25 units

 * FedEx: 20 units

 * NICA Container Freight Line: 20 units

 * DHL and J.B. Hunt

 As the high-volume production line ramps up, these orders serve as a massive vote of confidence for the future of electric heavy-duty freight. Over 1000 $150K rebates were issued in California on top of all this. 

 Cost per Truck - Long Range (500 miles) costs $290,000 to $300,000
