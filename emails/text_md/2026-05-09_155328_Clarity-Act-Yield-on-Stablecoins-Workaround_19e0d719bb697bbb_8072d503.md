 This is great news. When I first saw it I was like how are they doing this... the Banksters want to KILL YIELD on Stablecoins to preserve they non interest bearing deposits... but then this. 

 What is SWEEP? It’s basically a super-fast, always-open high-yield cash account — but built on blockchain. State Street (a giant $5T traditional finance company) and Galaxy launched a tokenized fund on Solana that lets big investors “sweep” their PYUSD (PayPal stablecoin) into it. The fund invests in safe U.S. Treasuries and pays yield, with 24/7 instant deposits/withdrawals in PYUSD thanks to Solana’s speed.

 My Clarity Act Confusion? The law stops stablecoin companies from paying interest directly on PYUSD (to protect banks). BUT this fund is separate — the yield comes from real Treasuries, not the stablecoin itself. So it’s allowed and compliant.

 Is it good for Solana? hell Yes — very bullish. A massive traditional Wall Street player picking Solana first (before Ethereum) is a huge vote of confidence. It brings real institutional money, legitimacy, and more tokenized funds to the network. This kind of adoption usually drives higher usage and demand for SOL.
