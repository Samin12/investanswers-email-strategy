 AI progress is hitting real-world limits — especially power and construction speed. OpenAI is also hitting that wall for compute. 

 OpenAI’s big plan to build its own massive AI “super brain” factories (called Stargate) has stalled completely. Elon said there was no funding lat year and he was correct. 

 By early 2026, the project had turned into a mess. The partners fought over who would be in charge. It was extremely expensive and risky to build everything themselves, so lenders wouldn’t give them the money. Most importantly, there wasn’t enough electricity and grid power available — the #1 bottleneck for all big AI projects.

 They missed their own big target for how much computing power they wanted online by the end of 2025.

 Now OpenAI is scrambling. Instead of building everything themselves right away, they’re renting a lot more computing power from cloud companies (Oracle, Microsoft, and even Google) to keep moving forward. And Google is even out of compute. 

 He or she who has the most compute wins!
