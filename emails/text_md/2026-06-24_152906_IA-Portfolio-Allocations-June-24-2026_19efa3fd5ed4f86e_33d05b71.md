 Well the summer doldrums have arrived and many names are taking a hit the last 2 weeks. 

 STRC is back near where it was 18th June. 

 MSTR under $100. 

 BTC approaching $60K for the 3rd time. 

 My Crypto Allocation (excl STRC) based on all this is now 21%. 

 AI Allocation is the balance (excl STRC) is now 79%. 

 A few interesting facts and Lessons for us all A few years ago, over half of my investment portfolio (56%) was in MicroStrategy (MSTR). It grew from almost nothing because the stock rocketed from $16 to $450, and I was using options (which multiply your gains).

 Over time, I slowly sold off my MSTR to buy Tesla (TSLA) instead. I did this in 25 separate trades, which I posted about here as they happened.

 Now, over half of my portfolio (54%) is in Tesla. This turned out to be a great move because, since then, MicroStrategy has lost 82% of its value compared to Tesla. and Rem in 2017 - 2020 BTC was my money market, then 2021 to 2024 MSTR was my Money Market... since then TSLA is my Money market - (low downside high upside)

 My only mistake was not selling every single share of MicroStrategy. I kept some for two reasons:

 Taxes: Selling the rest would have given me a massive, incredibly expensive tax bill from all the profits.

 Bitcoin: MicroStrategy's value is closely tied to Bitcoin. I didn't own much actual Bitcoin, so holding onto some MicroStrategy was my way of staying invested in crypto.
