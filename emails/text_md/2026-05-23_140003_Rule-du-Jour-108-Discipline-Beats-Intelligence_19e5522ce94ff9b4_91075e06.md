 A team of researchers in New Zealand followed 1,037 babies from the day they were born for the next 45 years to find out what actually determines a successful adult life, and the strongest predictor they found had almost nothing to do with intelligence or family wealth. It is self control. Not IQ. Not family income. Not parenting style. Self-control. Simple keys are ability to wait, regulate frustration, follow instructions, and resist impulsive reactions. That is IT! LINK to PAPER 

 The ability to delay gratification and stick with long-term decisions predicts real wealth far better than intelligence or starting money. Train your self-control like a muscle — practice choosing what you want most over what you want right now.

 The Details Your long-term financial success is not determined by your IQ, your ability to predict the market, or how much money you started with. It is determined by a single measurable trait: your self-control.

 The ultimate edge in investing is the ability to consistently choose what you want in ten years over what you want right now .

 How to apply it: Stop trying to be smart: You don't need a genius strategy. You just need the discipline to stick to a boring one. Find your rhythm as I say. 

 Ignore the impulse: The urge to sell during a panic, chase a top, or the shiney new stock or crypto, or blowing yourcapital today is the enemy of compounding.

 Train the muscle: Patience isn't a personality type; it's a muscle. Every time you delay gratification and choose your future self over your present impulse, your financial success becomes mathematically more certain.

 How I apply it in my life: eg each day I fast, I have dinner at 6pm and I do not eat until my video the next day is complete - often around 1pm or 2pm. The hunger motivates me to deliver. This is daily extreme "Self-control" 

 eg default answer to buying anything is NO.... I need to prove to myself why it is good after deep research. I ignore the herd and have to prove to myself. 

 eg I never FOMO, I Force myself to wait for a pullback to a high-probability support level. eg took me nearly 10 mths to build my IA13 bag.
