 We're in the middle of a historic shift. The economy used to run on land, workers, and money/machines. Now it runs on data and AI. Tech companies are going all-in on this new reality by spending enormous sums to build the infrastructure for it.

 And because this shift affects everything (your job, your investments, the products you use, even how governments and hospitals work), we're all effectively becoming AI investors — whether we like it or not.
