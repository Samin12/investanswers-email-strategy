 SpaceX (Elon Musk’s rocket and satellite company) is planning to go public soon with what could be the biggest IPO in history.

 It wants to start trading on the Nasdaq stock exchange (ticker: SPCX) as early as June 12.

 The company is aiming to raise about $75 billion at a $1.75 trillion valuation.

 This is a big jump from its previous $1.25 trillion valuation. Also rumors of $2 trillion

 The IPO could price as soon as June 11, with the official process starting next week.
