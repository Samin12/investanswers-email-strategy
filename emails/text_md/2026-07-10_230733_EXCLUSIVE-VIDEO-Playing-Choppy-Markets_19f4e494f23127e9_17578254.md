 Jacob recaps the week of Jul 6 - Jul 10, 2026: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iPzsJ6REvj8 

 Simple Core Rules 20-day line break = big upward move is usually over (especially in semis). Expect 2–6 months of sideways chop, not a quick V-shaped recovery.

 200-day line = best place to buy quality stocks on dips.

 50-day line = near-term trend. Reclaiming it after a drop is a good sign.

 Use ATR levels to spot specific buy zones on pullbacks.

 Now is mostly accumulation time — slowly buy dips on good names instead of chasing big moves.

 Ticker-by-Ticker How-Tos SPCX (SpaceX) 

 Strong support expected at 135 (lots of buyers who missed the IPO).

 If it keeps dropping, check the 4-hour chart with trend tool for a reversal entry.

 Big upside targets: $205–$300 in a year. (James bought at $145 - his first bite - 1/20th of his plan)

 QQQ 

 Reclaimed the 50-day line → near-term positive.

 Semiconductors (MRVL, ARM, AMD, AVGO, MU, NVDA, TSM, ALAB, SMH) 

 Most broke the 20-day line → momentum phase over. Expect sideways for months.

 Buy quality names near the 200-day line on dips.

 Use ATR levels for entry zones (e.g., MU near 770 would be strong).

 AMD is strongest — watch for breakout above all-time highs after more sideways.

 ALAB : Good buy zone 330–350 .

 PLTR 

 Choppy after big run up from the sub $107 buy. Chopping now. 

 TSLA 

 Chopping — needs to break 200-day line for confirmation.

 Can slowly accumulate here, but better confirmation above 420–430 (targets back toward 470s).

 Making higher lows — mildly bullish structure. Watch cybercabs. 

 BTC 

 Sideways, 50-day is resistance.

 Can start light buying/stacking now.

 Watch ATR level 2 if it breaks lower. Risk if MicroStrategy sells more.

 HYPE (Hyperliquid) 

 Strongest chart: series of higher lows . Break above 75–76 opens door to 100.

 Two simple ways to trade :

 Buy dips to the 50-day line, stop below it.

 Buy the breakout above resistance with tight stop just under (low risk, ride the move).

 Set alerts. Speaker thinks it’s only a matter of time.

 IGV (Software ETF) 

 Building higher lows — constructive.

 Break above 200-day line = bullish for software.

 GOOGL 

 Watch ~320 (200-day support). Earnings July 22.

 Other Quick Notes 

 META & AAPL : Positive moves, potential new highs.

 Quantum stocks (incl. INFQ) : Disappointing so far — wait for more news/catalysts.

 Oil : Downtrend, but possible bounce if Iran tensions rise.

 Key earnings to watch : ASML (Jul 15), GOOGL & TSLA (Jul 22).
