 Marvell (MRVL) surges after Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang called it the “next trillion-dollar company” during a casual onstage chat with Marvell CEO Matt Murphy at Computex.

 Huang highlighted Marvell’s essential role in AI data centers — specifically its networking chips, custom silicon, and optical tech that move massive amounts of data between thousands of chips.

 What makes MRVL special? Data center business now makes up ~76% of Marvell’s total revenue (up from much lower levels a couple of years ago).

 Nvidia invested $2 billion in Marvell earlier this year.

 Pre-surge market cap was ~$192 billion. Reaching $1 trillion would still require roughly a 5x increase.

 Huang’s comment lit the stamp of approval for Marvell to spike. MRVL has gone from basically nothing in my portfolio to 2.7%.
