 The event here is buzzing about SpaceX right now вАФ and for good reason.

 I recently spoke with several major LPs who hold very large positions. Here's what I'm hearing:

 SpaceX has a new targeted valuation in the $1.8 trillion range and a raise $80 billion

 Insiders are talking about 20+ lines of business across the combined entity. Items like asteroid mining ambitions are very real. 

 Total addressable market (TAM) at an eye-watering $28.5 trillion 

 AI compute is the big story here.... EWS coherent compute is mispriced. 

 Revenue growth is expected to explode over the next 3 years as Starship scales and new verticals open up. We've not even started this business yet. 

 A big point for existing LPs: Many are subject to extended lockups вАФ reportedly well beyond the standard 180 days, most exceeding 366 days from the IPO date. That means limited near-term selling pressure from early investors.

 On the AI side - many see spaceX eclipsing Anthropic in a year. This busienss alone will be worth over $1T. 

 IPO Demand is ferocious. Big institutional money is reportedly lining up with massive orders for the IPO day. Some conversations suggest it is 12x oversubscribed (double-digit multiples discussed in certain circles). The strategy for some large players like Blackrock: Get in big at the IPO, then rotate into passive/index flows as SpaceX gets added to major benchmarks (S&P 500, Nasdaq-100, etc.). 

 If you're a retail or smaller investor trying to get shares on day one, you might be lucky to receive a tiny fraction of what you order вАФ or nothing at all. Eg order 100 shares you may only get 1. I heard this from a top 6 bank. 

 On the allocation front, SpaceX recently disclosed it is reserving up to 5% of the IPO shares for employees and friends & family of executives. 

 It will be interesting to watch and despite the HUGE BEARISH sentiment out there re spaceX the big money insiders remain massively bullish and see this as being a huge IPO going to 2.3 TRILLION within days of the IPO.
