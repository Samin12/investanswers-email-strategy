 Zero Legacy Auto in the Top EV Brands - ZERO - Just Tesla and Chinese Brands. 

 Ford, GM, and Honda are basically done. Even Toyota is fading fast. The reason is simple: they're still mostly making gasoline cars in a world that's rapidly switching to electric vehicles — and they're losing badly on both technology and cost.

 The future of cars belongs to Tesla, China's EV makers. Gas-powered cars are starting to feel outdated for everyday driving.

 The switch isn't happening because of climate worries. It's happening because EVs are dramatically cheaper to run and maintain and not to mention FAR SAFER. No more oil changes, fuel filters, or engine repairs. You can even charge them from sunlight and cut your fuel costs to almost zero.
