 Who Are the NEW Magnificent 8? Most of the list is AI software, fintech, or consumer internet. SpaceX and Anduril stand out because they're "hard tech" — they're building real physical things. Cool to see 2 of the IA13 on there. 

 SpaceX — Rockets, Starlink, Starship, EWS/Compute, Frontier Models, Space Based Data Centers, Chips, Asteroid Mining AND the one pushing humanity toward being multi-planetary

 Anduril — Defense tech company building AI-powered autonomous systems, drones, and sensors, surveillance, border systems, etc.

 Stripe — The plumbing behind a huge chunk of online payments

 Anthropic — Major AI lab (Claude AI)

 Databricks — Data + AI platform used by tons of big companies - DATA DATA DATA.... WE NEED TO GET SOME DATABRICKS

 OpenAI — The ChatGPT/GPT company

 Revolut — Popular European fintech/digital banking app
