 Jacob recaps the week of May 18-22, 2026: youtube.com/watch?v=EMD255K1Um4 

 AI infrastructure names had huge moves earlier but some are now consolidating or pulling back after earnings. Two themes caught fire: Quantum (on $2B government funding news) and Space (SpaceX IPO excitement). Crypto miners are also ripping as many pivot toward AI. Broad market (S&P and Nasdaq) is still grinding higher near records. Bitcoin broke $76k but is digesting gains.

 Standout Stocks

 ALAB (Astera Labs): Massive +41% week. Huge breakout candle past $200 on strong AI demand. Host expects more upside (possibly toward $300) but says a normal pullback is likely after such a violent move. Sector momentum is very strong.

 ARM: Even bigger winner at +43%. Clean breakout above the key $180 level and just kept running. Core AI chip design play that’s working well.

 NVDA (NVIDIA): Down 6% after earnings beat. Classic “run up into earnings, sell the news.” Fundamentals still look excellent and they’re guiding higher. Host’s buy zone: anything under $200.

 MRVL (Marvell Technology): Up 12%. Choppy consolidation after a big run. 

 MU (Micron): Recovered nicely from a 20% pullback. AI memory demand remains solid.

 PLTR (Palantir): Showing early signs of turning. Six straight green days and holding support near $125. Short-term chart is improving. Decent entry zone in the low $130s; even better if it dips toward $100–110.

 TSLA (Tesla): Reclaimed the 200-day moving average after testing it. Structure looks positive for a potential run toward $450. 

 AVGO (Broadcom): Lagging peers so far. Key level to watch is $400 support. Break above $430 could open a move toward $500. Longer-term investors may like pullbacks into the $350–380 zone.

 AMD: Pushing higher but failed to make a new high today. There’s a big gap around $360 that could get filled on weakness. Some trimming visible.

 GOOGL (Alphabet): Rare sell signal on the daily chart. Possible dip toward $340 or the 50-day average as a mean-reversion buy spot.

 ASML: Finally broke out to new all-time highs and is holding. Clean and positive.

 TSM (TSMC): Quietly grinding higher. Not much to do here right now.

 Quantum Theme (Hottest Story of the Week)

 Trump administration announced a $2 billion investment in quantum computing. This triggered big volume and sharp moves as money rushed in.

 INFQ (Infleqtion): Gapped up hard with 4x normal volume — clear new interest.

 RGTI (Rigetti): +19% in one day on 5x volume.

 QBTS (D-Wave): +14% on 3x normal volume.

 Crypto & Bitcoin

 Bitcoin (BTC): Broke downkey line in the sand is $65k — a break below that would be bearish. After a 26% rally, some digestion is normal. Needs to hold ~$71–74k and eventually reclaim the 200-day moving average for bulls to regain control.

 Solana (SOL): Pulled back to ~$85 after a good run. Needs to break $100 then $110 (200-day). Important signal: the 200-day itself needs to start rising (not just price above it) for a real bull market confirmation.

 Markets are closed Monday for the holiday — enjoy the long weekend!
