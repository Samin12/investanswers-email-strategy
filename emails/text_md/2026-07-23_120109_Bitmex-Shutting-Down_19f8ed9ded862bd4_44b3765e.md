 Another Crypto exchange bites the dust. 

 BitMEX, the exchange Arthur Hayes co-founded in 2014, will shut down Sept. 23 following a "strategic review" by platform operator HDR Global Trading.

 New registrations are already halted. Risk limits kick in Aug. 26, and any positions still open at closure get force-closed. Withdraw if you use it, I do not know anyone who does.
