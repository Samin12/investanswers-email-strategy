 Rem this is a Threshold some believe in but there are ways around it. 

 I know “F u money” sounds rude and crude… but it’s actually a real financial term that’s been around for decades. It’s intentionally blunt because the whole idea is aggressive: this is the amount of money that gives you total freedom. Enough so you never have to be polite, compliant, or stuck in a situation you hate ever again. You can walk away from a bad boss, a toxic job, or anything else and just say “nope” with zero fear.

 The phrase got a huge boost from a famous movie scene, and the FIRE movement (Financial Independence, Retire Early) really ran with it in the 2000s and 2010s.

 F u Money/Regular FI / Lean FI = enough money to cover a basic lifestyle for the rest of your life without working.

 The table I just showed is the level you need to be able to retire at pretty much any age and live well.

 There’s one big caveat though (I’ll cover it in a video later today). Things are quietly shifting in society right now… and no, it’s not AI.

 Bottom line: Retiring in Switzerland or Singapore? Forget it — way too expensive. Spain? Looking pretty good.
