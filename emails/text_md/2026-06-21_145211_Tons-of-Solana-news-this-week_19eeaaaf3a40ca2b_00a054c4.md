 Before we jump in, just wanna note if any other chain had as much going on I would report that also.... 

 First - new inflation measure. Current inflation is roughly 3.8%. A new proposal (SIMD-0550, submitted early June 2026 by a Helius engineer, building on earlier SIMD-0411) would double the disinflation rate from 15% to 30%. It has 75% chance of passing. Effects if passed: Reaches the 1.5% terminal rate in 2.8 years (by early/mid-2029) instead of 5.7 years.

 Moody’s (the big credit rating company) started giving official credit ratings to assets on Solana. This is a big deal because it helps make tokenized stocks, bonds, and other real-world assets more trustworthy for big investors.

 On the tech side, Amazon’s AWS cloud service started accepting Solana stablecoins for payments — specifically to help monetize AI traffic. And Kraken added Solana DEX trading directly into their main app, making it much easier for regular users to trade on Solana without leaving the exchange.

 Trading in these tokenized stocks and ETFs went crazy — Solana hit an all-time high with over $100 million in volume in just one week. Ondo listed 173 new stocks and ETFs, and Backpack’s tokenized SpaceX token gained serious traction, crossing 10,000 holders.
