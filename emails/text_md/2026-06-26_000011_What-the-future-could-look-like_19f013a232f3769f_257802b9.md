 When I look around and see the Tesla community frustrated and turning on itself right now - I know why... it ie because everybody is frustrated and upset right now - WEN CYBERCAB etc... weird ideas like Elon is keeping Tesla suppressed so SpaceX can buy them cheap etc.... it is all nonsense - but I want to share my thoughts with an analogy. 

 MICRON vs TSLA/SPCX Micron’s story is historic because it shows a dead old commodity was completely redefined by AI infrastructure needs—turning a commodity player into a high-growth, high-margin powerhouse in months...... I have never seen anything like it. But this is not my point. 

 Tesla and SpaceX are already leaders in their own disruptive domains (physical AI/robotics/autonomy for Tesla; reusable rocketry and space infrastructure for SpaceX). If they execute on their next big platforms (Robotaxi/Optimus for Tesla, Starship-enabled expansion for SpaceX data centers), they have all the ingredients for similarly dramatic growth phases—potentially even larger in scale given the TAMs involved. We have seen a glimpse of that w EWS going from zero to 30bn in hi margin rev per year in just 30 days... this is why I am positive and patient. 

 Remember they have no competition. All hi margin businesses like Micron Memory. But with the most unreal MOATS on Earth and going after the largest TAMS on Earth. Fun times ahead.
