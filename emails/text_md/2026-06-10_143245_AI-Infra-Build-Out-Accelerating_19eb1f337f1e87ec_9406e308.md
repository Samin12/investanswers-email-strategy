 Bullish on MU

 The Details Nvidia’s CEO Jensen Huang just said something very important that every AI investor needs to know. 

 He basically said the big rush to build AI systems is speeding up fast. The second half of this year is going to be much bigger than the first half, and next year is going to be huge .

 One company that’s in a great position to benefit from this is Micron . Every Nvidia AI chip needs special super-fast memory chips attached directly to it. This memory acts like high-speed fuel — it feeds data to the AI chip so it can work at full speed. Without enough of this memory, the AI chips can’t perform properly.
