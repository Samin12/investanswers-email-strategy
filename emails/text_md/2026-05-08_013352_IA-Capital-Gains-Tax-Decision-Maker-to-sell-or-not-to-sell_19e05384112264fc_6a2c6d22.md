 With so many of you making massive gains in just weeks or months (AMD, ALAB, MRVL , GOOG etc the same question keeps landing: “Should I sell and take some profit?”

 My answer is always the same: It depends on the taxes.

 If it’s in a tax-free account (Roth IRA, HSA, etc.) → Yes, take profit. No tax drag.

 If it’s taxable and these are short-term gains (< 1 year) → the math changes completely. You’re looking at ordinary-income rates that can wipe out 50%+ of your profit.
