 Why This Slide Feels So Powerful - it shows AI as a full-stack transformation :

 Everything is connected. The power plant, the chip fab, the data center, the satellite network, the robotaxi, and the AI app on your phone are all part of one giant, interdependent system that’s being rebuilt and has AI at the base. 

 This slide gives a simple mental model for understanding where the biggest winners (and biggest capital needs) will likely be in the coming years. It’s not about picking one stock — it’s about seeing the entire profit-pool map that AI is redrawing.

 Standout Example from the Talk: Starlink & the $400 Billion Cell Phone Market The speaker made this point very directly about the TelCo → Starlink:

 The current global cell phone / mobile communications market has a Total Addressable Market (TAM) of $400 billion.

 But Coatue’s view is that Starlink is not just competing inside that $400 billion market — it is going after something much larger. By providing fast internet from space, Starlink can:

 Reach places traditional cell towers can’t (remote areas, oceans, skies, developing regions)

 Support the explosion of AI devices, sensors, autonomous vehicles, and connected machines everywhere

 Dramatically expand the overall connectivity opportunity

 Starlink is positioned to capture or create trillions in value, not just hundreds of billions. It’s a category-defining play on global connectivity, not a niche satellite business.
