 Elon just dropped this on X:

 “I think SpaceX might be able to reach approximately $1 trillion in revenue by 2030.”

 Last year SpaceX did $18.7 billion in total revenue. Elon is saying they could do $1 trillion in just five years. That’s roughly 50x growth. Not 50% bigger. Fifty times bigger.

 For context, the Wall Street banks that helped take SpaceX public were already using some very bullish numbers to justify the huge valuation:

 Morgan Stanley: $330 billion by 2030

 Goldman Sachs: $474 billion by 2030

 These are already massive numbers. But Elon is basically saying: “Even you guys are still underestimating this.”

 And the big reason comes down to one thing the banks are modeling… but apparently not modeling aggressively enough: space-based AI compute.
