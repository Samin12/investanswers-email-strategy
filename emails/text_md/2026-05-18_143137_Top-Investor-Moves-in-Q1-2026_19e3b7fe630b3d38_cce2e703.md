 Ackman saw Microsoft as attractively priced after a drop and used the opportunity to buy.

 Other items

 A lot of these big investors trimmed or sold some of their big tech stocks (especially Microsoft, Nvidia, Google, etc.).

 They didn’t all sell everything and run away. Many of them just moved their money around — selling some things and buying others.

 It’s like rebalancing a big investment portfolio after tech stocks had a huge run-up.

 Harvard’s endowment completely sold ALL their Ethereum (crypto) investment. They bought the Ethereum recently and sold it at a loss (roughly 30-35%).
