 Fun thought experiment... and rem the top 0.3% of stocks return over half of all returns and that is going to be even more extreme per my AGI forecasts. Anyhow back to the numbers

 If you invested $100 in American stocks at independence you would have $238 million today

 The CAGR on that is 3.6% per year...not much but accelerated a lot the last 40 years (Tech). 

 If you modify for Dollar Debasement - modest return at 2.1% - the real money printing didn't really happen until the last few decades.
