 Hong Kong has overtaken Switzerland as the worldвҖҷs largest cross-border wealth hub, driven by a surge of mainland Chinese capital and a recovery in the cityвҖҷs equity markets, according to Boston Consulting Group.

 BCG estimates that wealth managers in Hong Kong held $2.9tn in cross-border assets in 2025, around 60 per cent of it sourced from mainland China.

 How did CH fall behind? Switzerland client base is oriented toward Western European stocks, with less exposure to the fast-growing markets.
