 John Jumper is a top notch Google AI Engineer. Well he just quit to leave for Anthropic. 

 He co-led the development of AlphaFold at Google DeepMind — the groundbreaking AI system that solved the long-standing “protein folding problem.” It can accurately predict the 3D structure of proteins from their amino acid sequence. In 2024, he shared the Nobel Prize in Chemistry with Demis Hassabis (DeepMind CEO) for this work.

 He joined DeepMind around 2017 (shortly after finishing his PhD) and spent nearly 9 years there, eventually becoming a Vice President.

 Now at Anthropic. I hold a google position as part of the IA13 and was hedged but closed it at a profit. I hedged due to the ATR model above but my mistake was NOT HEDGING LONGER or DEEPER. Sigh. 

 Google is 0.8% of my Portfolio. 

 PS I feel bad for Berkshire Hathaway... buying the top. Their first foray into Tech since Apple. Warren would never have done that.
