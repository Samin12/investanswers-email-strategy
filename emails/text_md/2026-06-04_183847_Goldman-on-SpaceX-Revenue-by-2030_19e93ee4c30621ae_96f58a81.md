 Financial experts at Goldman Sachs think SpaceX is about to become a massive player in Artificial Intelligence Infra (AI hyperscaler ie EWS), not just space travel.

 The Numbers 

 Right now, SpaceX's AI services make aproox $15 billion + a year (incl the new Anthropic deal). In just five years, experts predict that number will skyrocket to $322 billion. That is 22 times bigger than it is today.

 By the year 2030, SpaceX is expected to bring in $474 billion overall. AI will be generating the vast majority of that cash—making even more money than their rockets and Starlink internet combined.

 TradFI narrative is changing 

 Instead of just seeing SpaceX as a "rocket company" that launches things into space, investors are starting to see it as a giant tech company that builds the AI INFRA on top of global comms.
