 Great reminder of why I built the IA13 as I was following the money... this is a cool chart on the current breakdown of what it takes to build a one gigabyte data center, which are very few and far between on the planet. But either way it will cost at least $50 billion today to build one (if you even can given regulations, shortages of power, transformers, chips and memory) and who knows a year or two from now that could be $100 billion. 

 What is also interesting is 28% of the cost is chips and 15% of the cost is memory... both making up nearly half. 

 Now the question is how much revenue can you generate with a gigawatt data center? Well Elon and SpaceX can probably churn $30BN out of a 1 Gigawatt Data center, but many other hyperscalers struggle to get $8 to $18bn out of one. 

 The breakdown of my nearly $30BN - is because SpaceX also use their own compute for Grok/Cursor etc

 Anthropic now pays SpaceX $1.25 billion every month

 Google pays $950 million a month

 Reflection AI pays $150 million a month.
