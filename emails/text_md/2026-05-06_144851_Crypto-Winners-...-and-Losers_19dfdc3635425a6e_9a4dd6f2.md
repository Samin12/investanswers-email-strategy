By Q1 2026, the picture is completely different: Solana leads at 27%, Tron is right behind at 25%, Ethereum has fallen to 18%, BNB at 13%, with others much smaller or NON EXISTENT... WINNERS TAKE MOST as I continue to hammer home.

 Note Tron is all (~90%+) revenue comes from USDT (Tether) transfers in Asia — not DeFi, NFTs, gaming, or complex smart contracts.

 Solana was basically invisible in revenue share back in Q4 2022 (~1%). Now it’s the clear leader at 27% despite trading at only 17% of ETH market cap.
