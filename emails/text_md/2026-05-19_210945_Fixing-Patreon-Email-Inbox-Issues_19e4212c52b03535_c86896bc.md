 Some of you have reached out regarding no longer receiving Patreon emails. As of May 5, Patreon emails are now being sent from a new sender address: investanswers@creator.patreon.com . This change may have resulted in emails landing in your spam folder or filtered in other inbox areas (such as the promotions tab). If this happened to you, please read on.

 ACTION: please add investanswers@creator.patreon.com to your approved sender address. Check your spam folder or promotions tab to see if emails have filtered there. 

 Send a message to us or Patreon support if you are experiencing any ongoing issues.

 Tips for Gmail users:

     a.    Go to Gmail Settings (gear icon) → See all settings → Filters and Blocked Addresses → Create a new filter.

     b.    In the "From" field, paste investanswers@creator.patreon.com 

     c.    Click Create filter.

     d.   Check this box(es):

     ◦    Never send it to Spam

     ◦    Always mark it as important (optional)

     ◦    Apply the label → create a new label called "Patreon" (only if you want to filter them into a new label)
