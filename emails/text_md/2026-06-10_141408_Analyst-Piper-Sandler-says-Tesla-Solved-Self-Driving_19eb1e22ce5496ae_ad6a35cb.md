 Piper Sandler believes Tesla 'has solved the self-driving puzzle' 

 Piper Sandler analyst Alexander Potter notes that when it comes to the efficacy of Tesla's full self-driving software, there's no shortage of skeptics. In meetings, clients frequently reference Waymo's greater robotaxi deployment numbers as a reason to doubt Tesla's readiness. 

 Nonetheless, the firm thinks Tesla has effectively achieved Level 4 autonomy given it is promoting insurance, it is building Cybercabs with no steering wheels or pedals, it is seeking permits to build robotaxi infrastructure, it is disclosing FSD subscriptions which are ramping fast, and it is expanding its robotaxi service. Furthermore, analyst Alexander Potter says that in April, his Tesla drove him from Missoula to Minneapolis, thus reinforcing his conviction. Piper has an Overweight rating on the shares with a price target of $500.

 Note also unsupervised Robotaxis grew by 10 more cars last nite.
