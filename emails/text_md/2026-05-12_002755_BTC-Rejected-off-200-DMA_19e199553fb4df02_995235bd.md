 The 200-day moving average is one of the most important long-term trend filters in Bitcoin - eal simple:

 Above the 200 DMA → Bullish regime. The line acts as dynamic support (a floor). Pullbacks to it are typically bought aggressively. This is the normal state during a bull market.

 Below the 200 DMA → Bearish regime. The line flips to dynamic resistance (a ceiling). Rallies into it are usually sold. This is the normal state during a bear market or major correction.
