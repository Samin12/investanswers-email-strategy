 Welcome to the quiet engine behind generational wealth. 

 The ultra-rich solved this generations ago:

 Buy scarce, appreciating assets (Tesla, SpaceX, real estate, Bitcoin, etc.).

 Borrow against them tax-free using securities-backed lines of credit (SBLOCs).

 You keep the shares, the upside, and the votes.

 Loan is not income → no capital gains tax.

 Never sell (if you can help it). Let inflation slowly debase the fixed debt while the asset rises in nominal dollars.

 Your debt shrinks in real terms. Your collateral grows. You stay long real assets and short the dollar.
