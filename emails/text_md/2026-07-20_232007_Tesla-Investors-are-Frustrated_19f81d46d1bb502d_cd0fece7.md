 I did not buy shares today as the chart looked off, I was waiting a turnaround - but the real story is everyone is upset. I get it. But we've been here before I just think the expectations are extreme. 

 Why investors are pi$$ed Big promises keep slipping eg Robotaxi Rollout, Optimus Demo etc. 

 Heavy Capex Spend I reckon it could be $4bn this quarter which could turn FCF negative. 

 Elon said 2026 would be great and 2027 off the hook or words to that effect... well we are not seeing it. 

 I reckon they will beat Earnings Expectations on Wednesday (they have done 3 of the last 4 reports) but as usual it is all about the Elon Mood and Fwd Guidance. 

 Revenue: ~$27.5 billion (up from $25.5B last year)

 EPS: ~$0.44–$0.50

 We need solid updates on robotaxi ie when scale and Optimus production timelines. If we do not get that the market will be more upset. But also note the stock has been selling off into the earnings - this normally means we pop on Earnigs. We shall see.
