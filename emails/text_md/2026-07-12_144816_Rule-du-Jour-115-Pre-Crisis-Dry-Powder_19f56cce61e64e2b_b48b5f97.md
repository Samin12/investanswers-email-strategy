 InvestAnswers Rule: The Pre-Crisis Dry Powder Rule

 This rule turns is inspired by the BofA cash chart - I see it as a warning sign. Low cash cushions across the market = higher systemic risk in the system. Your job is to be on the right side of that dynamic.

 I always like to try hold 10%+ cash in my portfolio and dynamically raise that allocation as markets rise and valuations stretch. My caveman tell for this is when my account hits new ATH it is time to hit the brakes and raise cash or hedge (to raise cash also). 

 NOTE: Cash is not margin. Always maintain a separate buffer of unused margin capacity. 

 The real danger shown in the current chart (private client cash at just 10% of AUM вАФ near 20-year lows vs. the long-term ~12% average) is this: in a true black swan with a hard dip, leveraged investors hit margin calls with no cash available to meet them. That forces selling, which creates a further nasty downward spiral - ps I live for these moments. 

 The Mistake Per the chart it appears investors have cash during a crisis but this is not true. Most investors do not build cash before a crisis. They panic-sell during it once fear takes over ie they sell the bottom... the worst thing u can do. 

 The Secret - Pre-Crisis Dry Powder Rule The edge comes from doing the opposite: positioning yourself in cash in advance , then you are ready to deploy aggressively at the bottom when others are forced to sell.

 Core Takeaways of the Rule Minimum: 10% cash cushion (higher when markets are euphoric or expensive).

 Dynamic: Increase cash as markets climb вАФ donвАЩt wait for the drop.

 Margin discipline: Cash and margin buffer are two different things. Protect both. Eg my margin is driven by holdings such as Tesla - if Tesla drops hard so does my margin... rem this and take this into account when calculating downside. Me today, if you imagine my portfolio being $100, I am about $12 cash but on top of that I have $66 of Margin available to buy things like LEAPS and stock - but also rem if the market tanks that Margin Available also tanks. 

 Be the one with dry powder before the event, not the one scrambling after it starts.
