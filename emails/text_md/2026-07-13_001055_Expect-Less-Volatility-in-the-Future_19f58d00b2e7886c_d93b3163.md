 AI is going to make the stock market less wild and unpredictable in the years ahead.

 Day-to-day price swings should get smaller because AI can analyze information much faster and more accurately than humans. It won’t get emotional or overreact to headlines the way people do, and it will constantly fix small pricing mistakes as they happen.

 When there is a big panic-driven selloff, AI-driven money should step in quicker and more rationally, which could lead to faster recoveries instead of long, drawn-out crashes. This is one of the things I love doing..... sigh the AI could even front run me. 

 Individual stocks might also start moving more on their own fundamentals rather than just rising and falling with the overall market. That’s because personalized AI portfolios could gradually replace the big wave of money that currently flows in and out of broad index funds and ETFs.

 For people like me who actually enjoy buying when things look scary (the “running into burning buildings” trades), I probably won’t be as alone anymore. AI systems will likely spot a lot of the same opportunities I do. They’ll also get better at seeing where things are headed, similar to how we use our own price prediction models.

 Interesting times ahead either way.
