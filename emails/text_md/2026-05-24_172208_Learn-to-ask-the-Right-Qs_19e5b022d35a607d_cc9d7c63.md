 Years ago I was asked in a Q&A what is most important skill for your kids to learn. 

 I said teach them how to ask the right Questions because AI has all the answers. 

 Enter Marc Andreesen - he just said this

 The only real skill left in using AI is knowing what to ask. The models can do almost anything you can describe in plain English. The bottleneck lives in your own head.
