 It absorbed a hot US inflation report (the strongest in three years), a massive $10.5 billion options expiry that usually creates chaos, a quick flash crash that slammed it down to $58,000, record Bitcoin ETF selling, basically zero DAT buying and despite all of that, Bitcoin is sitting right back around $60,000. 

 If the cycle repeats - many are excited just 12 weeks left. Many are also waiting for that magical dip to $40K but $60K remains a strong magnet for now.
