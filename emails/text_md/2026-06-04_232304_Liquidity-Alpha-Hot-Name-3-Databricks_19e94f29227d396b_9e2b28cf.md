 Databricks - my #1 pick from all the Liquidity Alpha names I will go thru them one by one today... this is the third

 As I covered in a Note on Monday... SAAS has 4 parts and 2 are dead. But tkey key part that is very much alive and very key going fwd is SAAS INFRA which includes Data. 

 The truth is we will need 10x more data stored over next 3 years and nobody is ready for that. Databricks is massively undervalued. 

 Why Databricks is Probably a Must-Own Going Forward Databricks stands out as a core infrastructure winner in the AI era for these reasons:

 Data Explosion is Non-Negotiable for AI: AI agents and enterprise AI need all data in one place with context + memory. Databricks' lakehouse platform (unifying data lakes + warehouses) is built exactly for this вҖ” ingesting, governing, and processing massive structured/unstructured data at scale for ML/AI.

 Positioned in the MAG8 Elite Tier - see previous post on that: These are the winners that will take most and also pull in 80% of all funding. 

 Undervalued Relative to Growth: Enterprise data needs are accelerating (memory quintupling per user, agents rewiring workflows), but public markets may not fully price the private/early-AI leaders yet. 

 AI-Native Advantage: Unlike legacy databases, Databricks is optimized for AI workloads (Unity Catalog, Mosaic AI, etc.). As models become a "utility layer" and agentic apps emerge, the data platform becomes the moat вҖ” collecting the context/memory enterprises lack today.

 Broader Tailwinds: AI disruption is "impacting everything globally at once" with no superintelligence yet. Data infrastructure is foundational (before agents, networking chips, energy, etc.). 

 Risks Still private/pre-IPO (secondary access via Forge/Schwab products), high valuations in private markets, competition (Snowflake, etc.), execution on agentic layer etc etc

 Bottom line: In a world needing 10x data infrastructure to power AI agents, enterprise rewiring, and compounding winners, Databricks is a foundational "picks and shovels" play in the MAG8. Will be researching proxies.
