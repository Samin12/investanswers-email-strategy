 18.4% of GLOBAL WEALTH IS IN STOCKS

 In just the past year, global stocks added $32 trillion in value вҖ” a +23.6% jump. That's much faster than the long-term average.

 Since the low point during the COVID pandemic in 2020, stocks worldwide have more than doubled, gaining a massive $94 trillion (+131%).

 Right now, the total stock market is worth about 134% of the entire world's annual economic output (global GDP). That's the highest level ever recorded.
