 I made a video 3.5 years ago and I slammed Tangem... note we have a wallet expert on staff and he did the review. 

 But it was funny as some youtubers who promote Tangem aggressively came after me after that video.... i did not care.... my Philosophy is if it's ever pumped hard it's probably a scam.

 Anyhow, the crux was 

 IA Conclusion re Tangem Wallet Not BIP39

 no seed recovery 

 Cheap material

 Pumped by pumpers

 These “card designed” wallets have a legitimate use case as alternatives to your everyday wallet. They can easily be carried in your pocket. However, they are NOT A cold storage device. I would NEVER use them as your primary cold storage device to store your life savings. Use them to store your spending money. Eg starbucks cash 

 The Flaw Years later -Ledger’s Donjon security research team (Ledger’s in-house hardware security lab) published research on July 9, 2026, showing a laser fault injection (LFI) attack against Tangem hardware wallet cards. The attack allows an attacker who has physical possession of a Tangem card to reset its password and gain full control of the funds — without knowing the original password or needing a backup card.

 Be careful if you use Tangem as COLD STORAGE... it is for pennies only - ie coffee money
