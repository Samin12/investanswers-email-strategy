 I have never seen so much handringing over an IPO.... the haters are out in droves. 

 Are they right or are they missing the oppty at hand? 

 Some of the critique SpaceX only made about $19 billion last year but lost nearly $5 billion.

 It’s burning cash fast on big projects.

 This feels like the top of a tech/AI hype cycle (like the dot-com bubble), especially with other huge unprofitable companies also going public.

 Too much depends on Elon Musk — what if something happens to him?

 The stock could crash hard after the hype dies down.

 Everyone will dump the stock after IPO - some truth to this hence we need to time our layers correctly

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 They see a company losing money, valued more than many giant established businesses, and call it crazy.

 What I see The “losses” aren’t waste — they’re heavy spending to build something much bigger:

 Starlink (satellite internet) is already profitable and growing fast with millions of customers worldwide.

 Starship (the giant new rocket) is expensive to develop now, but if it works, it could make space travel dramatically cheaper. This would let SpaceX launch way more satellites, and open up entirely new businesses. 

 Data Centers in space... this is going to happen. 

 They’re also investing in AI infrastructure - nobody else has capacity but Elon does. Hence the huge Anthropic deal. Oh and that $15BN a year deal is something the bears do not even factor in. 

 Did i mention they are by FAR the #1 leader in space and they are leaping further ahead every single day! 

 SpaceX isn’t just another company — it has real advantages no one else has (reusable rockets that land themselves, largest coherent compute clusters, a huge head start on satellite internet). Early Tesla and Amazon also lost money for years while building something transformative, and patient investors who looked past the losses did very well.

 The high price looks scary on today’s numbers, but it’s betting on SpaceX becoming a foundational company for the next 20–30 years (global internet + cheap space access + new industries), not just what it earns right now. 

 I remain bullish... and rem THERE IS NOTHING BIGGER THAN SPACE....
