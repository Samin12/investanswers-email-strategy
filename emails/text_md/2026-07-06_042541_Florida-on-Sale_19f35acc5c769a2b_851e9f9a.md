 As of tonight, 1 in 7 homes for sale in America is in Florida — a state with 8% of the nation’s housing stock.

 The Florida for-sale market is flashing clear warning signs:

 45% of listings have taken a price cut (6.6 pts above the national rate)

 1 in 10 homes are selling for less than the original owner paid sometimes as far back as 2020. 

 The reason is simple: You cannot insure many of these homes due to hurricane risk — and when you cannot insure, you cannot get a mortgage. This has turned Florida into a dream market for cash buyers. Same issue in parts of CA also. 

 If you have cash, and are thinking of Florida... get ready to place some low ball bids... high odds you will get filled.
