 A few months ago, when I said xAI and Grok could catch up to or even beat OpenAI and Anthropic by the end of the year, a lot of people in here called it blind Elon fanboy talk.

 Turns out there’s real movement happening.

 The big development is the xAI Cursor partnership! AI Coding has the best product market fit of all AI tools and Anthropic leads, OpenAI second.... but for a while there Cursor was top dog but ran out of compute. Cursor is an AI coding tool that exploded from $100M to $2B in yearly revenue in just over a year. It became the favorite of serious developers because it doesn’t just chat with you. It has a smart “workflow layer” (called the agent harness) that understands your entire project, edits multiple files, and actually helps you get real coding work done.

 Cursor just released a NEW coding model that performs at the level of the two top models but costs much less. Rem cost is everything. Teaming up with xAI/SpaceX gives Cursor access to xAI’s massive supercomputer cluster (the biggest in the world) and lets xAI use Cursor’s powerful workflow system.

 Raw AI models are becoming more similar. The real advantage now comes from who has the best system on top of the model + huge computing power. xAI just got both. ALso note xAI never had an enterprise game... now they do. Final note on this... xAI have option to buy cursor for $60BN. 

 Simple Sum-of-the-Parts View If xAI + Cursor can reach performance levels comparable to Anthropic, the combined business could reasonably be valued in the $1.2 trillion range. That single move would also place SpaceX at roughly $800 billion on a sum-of-the-parts basis (which is stooopid cheap).

 Elon does not just have the most compute and control it all, he also just inserted the missing piece (the harness + enterprise distribution) at a time when pure model scaling is losing its edge. That’s how you change the valuation math quickly. xAI went from “behind” to holding a credible path in enterprise agentic coding with one of the largest compute clusters on the planet. That’s the real story.

 As I have also said for a year now

 At the end of the day he with the most coherent compute wins...

 This doesn’t mean xAI has already won. But it gives them a credible, fast path to close the gap in the areas that matter most right now — especially building useful AI that can actually code and work autonomously.

 The “Elon fanboy” crowd might want to slow down on the mocking. The chess pieces just moved.
