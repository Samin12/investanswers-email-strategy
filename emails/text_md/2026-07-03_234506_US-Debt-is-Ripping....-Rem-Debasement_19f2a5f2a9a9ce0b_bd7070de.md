The most immediate and painful impact is the erosion of purchasing power for everyday essentials. Because cash loses value over time, holding uninvested capital results in a guaranteed loss of purchasing power.

 Never forget your silent tax. A friend of mine has been sitting on $600K cash now for 6 years since he sold a condo. He is afraid to invest... each time I tell him what to buy he says... "oh it is too high now, I will wait for a pullback" he is nearly 60 and hyper conservative.
