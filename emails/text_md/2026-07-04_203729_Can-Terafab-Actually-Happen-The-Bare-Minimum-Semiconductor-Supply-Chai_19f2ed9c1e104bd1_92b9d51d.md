 So I've been thinking a lot about what I would take to pull off a TeraFab at scale. Obviously you can't have hundreds of moving parts or partners. So here is the bare minimum list you need to be able to pull off TeraFab and it could be worth investing in some of these companies if the scale of TeraFab is as big as Elon says it's going to be. 

 1. Raw Materials / Wafers (foundational, capacity-constrained oligopoly) 

 Silicon wafers: Shin-Etsu 

 2. Design / EDA + IP (near-duopoly moat) 

 Synopsys and Cadence (together ~73% of EDA market) 

 3. Wafer Fab Equipment вАФ Lithography 

 ASML has a near-total monopoly on EUV and the upcoming High-NA EUV tools needed for advanced nodes (<7 nm, and certainly for competitive 5/3/2/1.4 nm-class AI chips). 

 4. Process Technology & Know-How
