 For those of you who were around when Tesla crashed to that magical $106 — you’ll remember we went hard on out-of-the-money call options at the $140 / $150 / $160 strikes.

 (I almost never do this… only during those rare, crazily oversold moments when I’m trying to maximize every dollar of buying power.)

 A bunch of mine got exercised last year, but I still have a big chunk left. Many of them are rolling into June 2026 and December 2026 ie this year. 

 Quick heads-up if you’re still holding these LEAP positions: make sure you have enough margin ready to exercise them. That’s how you build your “mattress” — the cash cushion you can then use to sell puts against. Sanjay got caught out on this last year being LEAP HEAVY. 

 And that , ie My Mattress, my friends, is the real magic money-making secret.

 In addition, although a lot of the 140s and 150s and 160s converted because I had expiry late last year you can still see where the concentration in my positions. The lower the asset goes the harder I go (see extreme concentration of LEAPS 140-220). Now with Tesla 430 bucks you can imagine the return on some of these synthetic Longs. The 300's all expire Dec 2028 so lots of time on those to cook. 220's are all Dec 2027/2028

 Final note,,, if TSLA gets above $650 by August we could get a stock split amplifying these leap contracts even more. The BEAUTY OF LEAPS
