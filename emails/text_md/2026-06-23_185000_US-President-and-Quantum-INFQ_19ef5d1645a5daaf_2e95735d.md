 May 21st US President says he will invest in Quantum. 

 Today he just signed two Executive Orders to speed up America’s quantum computer race.

 One order pushes government, universities, and private companies to build a powerful quantum computer for scientific research by 2028.

 The other prepares U.S. security systems for the risk that quantum computers could break today’s encryption sooner than expected.

 This comes as the Commerce Department is awarding billions to quantum companies.

 Trump is going big on quantum. BUT WHY??? Trump and the Pentagon are worried that China is winning the quantum race — and if they get there first, America loses its biggest security advantage. America’s military secrets, government communications, and critical infrastructure are protected by encryption that normal computers can’t break. But a powerful enough quantum computer can crack it.

 China is pouring massive resources into quantum. If they succeed first, they could:

 Read years of stored American secret messages (“harvest now, decrypt later”)

 Jam or spoof GPS that the military relies on

 Spy on or disrupt US satellites

 Gain a huge edge in any future conflict

 My Thoughts? Real simple, I think Quantum is far off..... 

 I think Good AI can do far more than Quantum can do today... in the future that remains to be seen. 

 Can Companies Like Infleqtion Matter make a difference? Infleqtion’s technology (which does both quantum computing and ultra-precise sensing) lines up perfectly with what the military needs for navigation, secure comms, and space systems. That’s why the stock jumped — the government is now treating this as urgent defense spending.
