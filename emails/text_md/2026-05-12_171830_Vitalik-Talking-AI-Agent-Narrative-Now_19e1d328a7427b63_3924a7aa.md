 Vitalik Buterin is saying that Ethereum can become the "money system" for AI.

 His POV Right now, most AI is controlled by big companies (like OpenAI, Google, etc.). But in the future, there will be thousands of different AIs — some made by companies, some by individuals, some running on their own. These AIs will need to talk to each other, work together, trade, and cooperate.

 The problem is how do they do that without fighting or one of them trying to cheat?

 Vitalik’s answer is a new economic layer — aka a blockchain:

 AIs can pay each other

 Make agreements

 Follow clear rules

 Build trust through money and incentives (instead of trusting each other personally)

 He believes Ethereum is the best place for this because:

 It’s decentralized (no single company controls it) - Nakamoto Coefficient of 2 ;o) 

 It already has strong rules for money and agreements (this is what “smart contracts” do)

 It can handle 15 TPS at a dollar a transaction.
