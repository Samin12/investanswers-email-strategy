 AVGO is ripping to new all-time highs in after-hours (pushing toward ~$476–480 from a $459.97 regular-session close) primarily because of fresh bullish news on Alphabet/Google’s massive AI infrastructure spending plans.

 Alphabet’s $80B Equity Raise for AI Today (June 1), Alphabet announced it is raising $80 billion in equity capital specifically to fund AI infrastructure and compute expansion. This includes:

 A $10 billion private placement to Berkshire Hathaway.

 Additional public offerings and at-the-market programs.

 This is a very strong signal that one of the largest hyperscalers is doubling down on AI capex. Broadcom is a key long-term partner for Google on this front:

 They have a multi-year deal (through 2031) to develop and supply custom TPUs (Google’s in-house AI chips) for future generations.

 Broadcom also supplies critical networking and other components for Google’s next-gen AI racks.
