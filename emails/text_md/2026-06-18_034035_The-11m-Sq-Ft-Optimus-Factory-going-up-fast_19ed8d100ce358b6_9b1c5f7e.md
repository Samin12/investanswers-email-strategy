 Tesla has made solid progress on the new Optimus factory at Giga Texas. They've finished the ground work and foundation supports, and now they're putting up the steel frame. In one section, it's already up to 4 floors tall.

 It looks like they're building it the same way they built the main factory. Some parts will probably be taller than others as construction continues. 

 They're also expanding the building further south by adding more land. Once it's finished, this new factory will be a pretty big chunk of the size of the main factory (which is 11 million square feet over 1 million sq meters). 

 It's turning into another massive building. They hope to be finished before summer 2027. Meanwhile Fremont will be up sooner and ramp to 1 million humanoids. This 11m sqft place will have capacity for 10 million humanoids per year.
