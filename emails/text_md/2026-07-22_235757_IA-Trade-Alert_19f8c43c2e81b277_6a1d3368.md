 I bought some Tesla Shares at $357. I added 1% to my holdings. I had been tempted to start deploying cash all week but the chart just looked weak as hell.... stock down hard in after hours from earnings call. 

 It was cash in an account. Straight equity no options. 

 The learnings I got from the call were net positive... Apart from the 20 lines, the key part that made me very bullish is Tesla 10% weekly compounding of Robotaxi - 7.3 wks to double, 1.75 doublings (almost 2) per qtr, 142x in 1yr. The question for Tesla is how long can they maintain a 10% weekly compounding rate?
