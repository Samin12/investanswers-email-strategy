 I met Mike Moritz a few times in the 1990s - he is a top VC from Sequioa. 

 He funded Google, Yahoo!, PayPal, YouTube, Zappos etc. and made his billions from that. 

 Now he said something profound in a far more eloquent way than I can ever express. 

 Addressing the valuation of TSLA and SPCX 

 legendary Silicon Valley investor Michael Moritz recently shared his perspective: “ There’s no PRECEDENT for the growth of some of these businesses [Tesla, SpaceX] . Similar things were written about the valuations of Facebook or Google or Microsoft. I remember when Microsoft went public and everybody thought the $400 million or $500 million valuation for the company was ludicrous.”

 When Wall St has no precedent it cannot appraise.
