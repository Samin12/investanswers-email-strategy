 Shares of Astera Labs closed up 13% on Tuesday. Why? 

 Astera Labs excited investors in a big way on Tuesday as management talked up its opportunity in the red-hot optical arena and offered more visibility into the timeline for a key networking switch. Executives delivered upbeat commentary at J.P. Morgan's Technology, Media and Communications Conference that their new Scorpio X fabric switches, a type of networking hardware used in data centers could plug into 90% of equipment in data centers. Astera Labs CEO Jitendra Mohan said Scorpio X will be the company's largest product by the end of the year.
