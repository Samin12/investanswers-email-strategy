 Crazy concentration in AI Stocks all of a sudden. Some stats:

 AI-linked stocks now make up ~39–45% of the entire S&P 500 by market value, according to Goldman Sachs and Deutsche Bank.

 Semiconductors are ~ 18%

 SPCX + TSLA ~ 8% 

 5 Years Ago (Mid-2021) Much less extreme - 

 Semiconductors low-to-mid single digits (~ 4–6% )

 True “AI stocks” (as we define them today) probably 15–25% at most. 

 How long it will last is as long as there is demand for Intelligence.... I dont see that slowing down.
