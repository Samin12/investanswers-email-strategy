 Broadcom is looking to clean up its balance sheet. They are looking to buy back some of its own older bonds from investors for cash. 

 They want to spend up to $2.5 billion to repurchase several series of their bonds (notes) that pay between 4.9% and 5.2% interest and mature between 2030 and 2038.

 This is called a tender offer — they’re making an offer to current bondholders to sell those bonds back to Broadcom early.

 AVGO looks attractive to me to add more low 300's - it is a beast and approaching the kill zone soon.
