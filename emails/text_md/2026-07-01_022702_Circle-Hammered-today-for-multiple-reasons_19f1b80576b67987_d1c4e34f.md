 Circle Stock down 16% today. 

 THere are multiple reasons 

 1) Apparently the Clarity Act is not going to happen this year.... this is hitting all of Crypto. 

 2) Stripe, Coinbase, Visa, Mastercard, BlackRock & 140+ others just launched a rival stablecoin called Open USD. It charges zero minting fees & hands the reserve yield back to partners. Circle's business model is the mint and the yield. 

 The issue w Stablecoins is it is all a race to the bottom. 

 Some say the banks dragged their heals on the Clarity Act to prepare their alternative.... at the end of the day Wall St likes to be in control and was never going to let Crypto run willy nilly on the future of finance. 

 Odds of Clarity being signed into law in 2026 tanked
