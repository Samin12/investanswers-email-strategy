 Accenture is in a tricky spot.

 They make huge money on the backs of 23 year olds helping companies use AI to improve and modernize their operations. But that same AI is also reducing the need for the traditional consulting and outsourcing work that used to be one of their biggest profit drivers.

 In short, they're selling a solution that's eating their lunch!
