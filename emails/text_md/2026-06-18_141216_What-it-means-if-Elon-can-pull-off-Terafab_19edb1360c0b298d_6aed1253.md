 Elon Musk has agreed to build Terafab, which will be designed together with Intel's technology team. Terafab will be BY FAR the largest chip plant on the planet built by both Tesla and SpaceX

 Musk announced the project in March, and Intel joined in April.

 The facility will aim to produce 1 terawatt of AI compute every year — roughly 50 times the world's current annual AI chip output. The first phase will cost about $25 billion, with the full build potentially reaching up to $119 billion.
