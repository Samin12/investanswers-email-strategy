 Interactive Brokers customers trade far more than everyone else — roughly 4x the trades per account compared to Robinhood, Fidelity, Schwab, or Morgan Stanley. These are the hyper-active traders.

 My Experience re trading frequency: This is exactly why 1 trade, maybe 2 per week is optimal for most people.

 Overtrading (buying and selling constantly) usually leads to losing money over time. You rack up fees, pay more taxes, make emotional mistakes, and get whipsawed by noise. The data and real-world results consistently show that lower-frequency, patient trading beats hyperactive trading for the vast majority of retail investors.
