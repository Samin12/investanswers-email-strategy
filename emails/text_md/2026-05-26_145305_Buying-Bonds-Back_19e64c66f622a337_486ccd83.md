 trategy (MSTR) took ~$1.4 billion from its cash reserve — money saved to pay high 11.5% monthly dividends on STRC preferred stock for the next 2+ years — and used it to buy back $1.5 billion of its own cheap 0% debt early at an 8% discount.

 Why they did it: Saves money on the buyback, slightly boosts their “BTC yield,” reduces total debt, and makes the balance sheet look cleaner. This helps credit ratings and could improve their chances of getting into the S&P 500 (which brings automatic index fund buying).

 The risk: They spent most of their dividend safety buffer. Now with far less cash on hand, if raising new money gets hard (stock dips, investors pull back), they may struggle to keep paying those expensive dividends without selling Bitcoin or heavily diluting common shareholders. It’s a bet that they can refill the cash quickly while Bitcoin keeps rising.

 Stock is up today while BTC is down.
