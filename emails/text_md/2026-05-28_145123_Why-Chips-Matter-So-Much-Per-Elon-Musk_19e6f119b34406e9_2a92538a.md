 100% of Advanced AI Chips are made in Taiwan, mainly TSMC. Elon has explained this creates a dangerous single point of failure. 

 If China invades Taiwan, the world gets cut off from these chips. The factories would likely be destroyed in the fighting (so China wouldn’t even get them). But global production of cars, AI, electronics, and more would collapse — a massive hit to economies and tech progress.

 Elon stresses that the US must rapidly build its own advanced chip factories (“fabs”) on American soil. This is the heart of the CHIPS Act and private efforts like big Texas projects.

 Why it matters: No chips no AI

 No chips no National security

 No chips no AI & no Robotics future (some political leaders would love this by the way)

 No chips no Economic or tech leadership

 As I have said before Chips are the new oil. A supply shock would hurt way worse than past energy crises.No wonder his entire focus is Tesla/SpaceXAI Terafab. 

 Note the SpaceX S-1 confirms scope but omits cost. SpaceXAi disclosed the Terafab plan with Tesla and Intel as partners, Intel's 14A process node, and a long-term 1 TW compute goal. The S-1 explicitly states capex is "not yet determined." The $55B, $119B and $5T figures circulating in the press come from a Texas tax-abatement filing and a Bernstein analyst note, not from SpaceXAi.
