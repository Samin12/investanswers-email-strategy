 TLDR: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Details The How To's for today

 PLTR (Palantir) – ATR Level 3 + Whole Number Reversal Setup The Magic Reversal Pattern (How-To) :

 Identify a multi-day sell-off (4–7+ consecutive red candles / big selling days).

 Wait for the first strong green candle .

 Confirm it breaks above the high of the prior day (or prior wicks).

 Check that price is also in/near the ATR Level 3 + $100 confluence .

 Traders : Look for reversal back to upside. Investors : ATR Level 3 + $100 becomes an interesting accumulation zone.

 MSTR (MicroStrategy) – Reversal Candle + BTC Divergence How-To for Similar Setups :

 Spot prolonged sell-off in MSTR (or other BTC-levered names).

 Check for divergence vs. Bitcoin (MSTR weaker = potential oversold relative to BTC).

 Wait for the reversal candle : green bar that breaks above prior day’s high after the sell-off streak.

 Look for chart patterns (buy triangles, etc.).

 Use the same multi-timeframe process: Daily for the setup, lower TF for entry.

 MU (Micron) – 20-Day MA Support in Strong Uptrend + Lower TF Confirmation How-To for Uptrending Stocks (Exact Steps from Video) :

 Confirm higher-timeframe uptrend (higher highs/lows, price well above longer-term MAs).

 On the daily chart : Mark the 20-day MA as your key area of interest after a rally or during pullbacks.

 When price approaches, touches, or wicks the 20DMA (MU did this and wicked right out), treat it as a higher-timeframe level of interest.
