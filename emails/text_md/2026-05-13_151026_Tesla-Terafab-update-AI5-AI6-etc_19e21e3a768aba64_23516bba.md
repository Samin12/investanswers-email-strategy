 There is a reason I am over 50% TESLA... actually there are 18 of them.... but this is one of them. 

 TSMC’s board just approved +$20 billion for its Arizona Fab 21 expansion and +$31.3 billion for future fabs to ramp AI-related capacity. I mean this is INSANE... TSMC are renowned for being super conservative on CAPEX and then they do this? WOW. 

 TSMC will produce AI5 chips initially in Taiwan, then shift to Arizona.

 AI6 will use TSMC’s 2nm process out of Arizona.

 Terafab itself is a massive, vertically integrated AI chip fabrication facility aimed at meeting “staggeringly large” internal demand for custom silicon (FSD, Optimus, Dojo, etc.) that external foundries alone can’t fully handle. 

 Tesla has repeatedly said they’ll keep TSMC and Samsung as key suppliers (they’re not trying to replace them entirely), but Terafab is the hedge for volume and speed. 

 If you read between the lines of all of this... it is MIND BLOWING. Future is bright. No other company on earth is doing this... not even close.
