 We are inundated w messages and Q's re missing the AI train..... You didn’t, and you didn't blow it. We’re just starting the next chapter.

 The AI capex boom and hyperscaler spending cycle are still in early-to-mid innings. Missing the first 2–3× on a few names doesn’t mean you missed the whole story — it just means you get to enter with better risk management and a clearer head.

 Here’s the no-BS game plan I wrote for a member who is feeling down.... and he also asked us to share. 

 1. Mindset Reset (Do This First) Stop beating yourself up. Regret is the most expensive position you can hold.

 Accept that you’re at a new starting line, not the finish line.

 Commit to playing the next 3–5 years, not the last 6 months.

 2. Choose Your Weapons Do NOT try to copy my spreads or option trades. Those are advanced tools for money I’m willing to lose. 

 Two clean paths only:

 Path A – Highest Conviction Growth (Recommended for most people) Pick 3–5 names from the IA13 basket that I’ve repeatedly called out as having the best risk/reward. You don’t need all 13. Over-diversification kills returns in a smaller portfolio anyhow.

 Path B – Income + Growth (If you want cash flow) Try buy 100+ shares of a name you like, then start learning covered calls. It’s the best “I already own the stock” strategy once you have the shares.

 3. Capital Allocation Rules (Non-Negotiable) Maximum 15–25% of your total investable capital into the entire AI theme. (I go heavier because this is literally my job. Most people should not.)

 Never go all-in on one narrative. Note my split between Crypto and AI. I also trade around the edges - eg Copper (also AI), short oil, short AVIS etc. 

 4. Practical Entry Plan (This is what actually matters) DO NOT BUY THE TOP system:

 Decide your total AI allocation (example: $50k out of $250k portfolio = 20%).

 Split that money into 4 equal tranches.

 Deploy over the next 3–6 months using one of these two methods:

 Steady DCA: Deploy one tranche every 4–6 weeks regardless of price. Use DCAS across different assets and wait for it to fire. It is like $19 and will save you millions. https://www.investanswers.io/product/dcas 

 Aggressive on weakness: Buy more aggressively on any 10-20% pullback in the names you like.

 Pro tips:

 Set price alerts on your 3–5 chosen names and stop staring at charts every day. Have TradingView on your phone 

 If a name you love is already up massively, start smaller (half a tranche) and add on weakness.

 Never chase. The market will give you chances. There is always somehting falling into a trap.... 

 5. The “I Own It Anyway” Income Layer (After You’re In) Once you own 100+ shares of a core holding:

 Learn covered calls.

 Sell monthly or weekly calls against your shares.

 This turns your position into a cash-flow machine while you still participate in upside.

 6. Long-Term Rules This is a multi-year theme, not a 3-month trade.

 Re-evaluate your 3–5 names once per quarter — not daily.

 If something fundamentally changes in one of your holdings, you can always rotate — but don’t panic-sell on normal volatility.

 Final Truth:

 You’re not “stuck.” You’re not late. You’re just early to the next leg.

 The fact that you’re still here, still learning, and still thinking clearly already puts you ahead of 90% of investors who either FOMO’d in at the top or gave up completely.

 Now go build the position the right way.

 You’ve got this.
