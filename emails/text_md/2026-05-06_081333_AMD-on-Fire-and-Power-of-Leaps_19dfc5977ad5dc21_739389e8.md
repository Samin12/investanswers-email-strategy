 AMD just dropped some monster earnings that crushed what Wall Street expected:

 They brought in $10.25 billion in revenue — up 38% from last year — beating the forecast of about $9.89 billion .

 Adjusted profit per share came in at $1.37 , beating the $1.29 estimate.

 Their data center business (the real growth rocket) hit a record $5.8 billion , up 57% from a year ago.

 A few weeks ago we bought long-term call options on AMD (LEAPS that expire in December 2028 with a $200 strike). After also selling a put to lower the cost, our net price was $14 .Stock was under $200 at the time. 

 Right after the earnings hit, AMD stock exploded in after-hours trading — shooting up a crazy $59 !

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 In just the last 35 days , AMD stock has climbed $220 — that’s nearly a 120% gain.

 Because of that move, our LEAP options are printing money:

 Just from the after-hours spike, they shot up 421% in after hours alone.

 Since we bought them a few weeks ago, the return is sitting at around 1,650% !

 Every 10 contracts controls $414,000 of Stock and the cost to control those 10 contracts was only $14,000 which these 10 contracts are $214,000 in the money. 

 And it’s not just AMD — the same crazy run is happening with MU, MRVL, ALAB, GOOG, and a bunch of other tech names. 2026 has been absolutely insane for these AI stocks.
