 The Simple AI Warning:

 When companies use tools like ChatGPT or Claude, they’re not just paying with money. They’re also feeding the AI their private business knowledge through every prompt, correction, and piece of feedback.

 That data reveals how the company actually works — and the AI provider could potentially use it to build competing products.

 This is why more businesses are moving to open-source AI they can run and control themselves, instead of handing their edge to big tech.

 In May 2023, Sam Altman told CNBC:

 “Customers clearly want us not to train on their data, so we’ve changed our plans: We will not do that.” Can we trust him? NOPE! He was already thinking about it.
