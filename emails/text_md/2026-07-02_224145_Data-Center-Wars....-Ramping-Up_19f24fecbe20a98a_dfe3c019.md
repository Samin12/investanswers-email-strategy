 Blackstone just walked away from its chunk of the planned world’s-largest data center campus in Virginia after years of lawsuits, community opposition over power/water use, and its partner bailing.

 AI data centers need city-scale electricity and land. Local pushback + grid constraints + permitting fights are slowing or killing projects in prime spots.

 Result: Terra based Data Centers and usable compute is getting scarcer and more expensive.

 Why this is even better for SpaceX: 

 They build best data centers the fastest

 They have the space optionality w Starship 

 Starlink delivers instant global connectivity for distributed/edge compute.

 The Musk ecosystem (xAI, Tesla MegaPod, in car inference, Megapacks, etc) is vertically integrated to own supply when everyone else hits terrestrial walls.

 PS this also makes bitcoin miners attractive

 Next 3 years will be very interesting.
