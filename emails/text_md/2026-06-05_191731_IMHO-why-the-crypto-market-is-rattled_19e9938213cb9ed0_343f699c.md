 TLDR I am not buying Crypto right now. The Zcash bug is not the main reason crypto is falling, the market was already weak, but it was a very bad psychological trigger at the worst possible time.

 WHY? Crypto runs on trust. The whole system only works if people believe the coins can’t be faked and the supply is honest.

 When news broke that one of the more technically respected privacy coins aka ZEC had a hidden “print money” bug for four years it made a lot of people think: “If they missed this for 4 years, what other hidden bugs exist in Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, etc.?” 

 ZEC cratered and now the fear is spreading.

 The crypto market was already falling hard this week for bigger reasons:

 Bitcoin ETFs have been bleeding money for days

 Broader risk assets were getting hit by the AI stock rotation

 Strong U.S. jobs data made people expect higher interest rates for longer

 IMHO the Zcash news acted like gasoline on the fire. It gave traders and algorithms another reason to sell.
