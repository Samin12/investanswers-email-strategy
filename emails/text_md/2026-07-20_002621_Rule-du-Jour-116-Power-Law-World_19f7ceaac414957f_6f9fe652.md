 In the power-law world, the average is pulled way up by a tiny number of extreme winners, while the median (typical outcome) sits much lower. The winners are the heavy right tail, extreme skewness. Most stocks deliver mediocre-to-poor results; it is only a small minority deliver the massive gains that dominate everything.

 This is the way of the universe and i keep stressing it. 

 Winners Take Most. Be in the Winners. 

 Everytime someone takes a flyer on an asset that is not in the top tier of assets will soon get hit. Flyers are allowed but make sure you only allocate crumbs. Eg in this chart of 500 firms... just 10 make up nearly half the value. The other 490 fight over the other half.
