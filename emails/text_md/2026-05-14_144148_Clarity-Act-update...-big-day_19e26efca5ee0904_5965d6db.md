 CLARITY UPDATE - we care because this will really pump things like BTC, SOL, CRCL etc

 The Senate Banking Committee is holding the markup (debate + vote on amendments) right now (started 10:30 AM ET). This is the key committee step to send it to the full Senate. Fresh bill text dropped a couple days ago after months of negotiations (stablecoin yield).

 Fidelity (the giant $7+ trillion asset manager) just publicly backed it hard — exactly what’s in your screenshot. They called it a “balanced approach” that keeps the U.S. a global leader in digital assets. Coinbase, Ripple, and others are also pushing for it. This kind of mainstream finance support is a huge signal.

 Short term: High chance it advances out of committee today (GOP has the majority there, and momentum is strong). 

 After that, full Senate vote, possible House-Senate tweaks, then President’s desk. White House and key senators are aiming for something like a July 4 signing if it keeps moving.
