 Trading and Investing: 

 Bubble Market: The easy way to think about the AI Bubble, there can be no bubble when bottlenecks exist.

 PLTR:

 IA sees PLTR hitting $180 to $200 in the next 6 to 12 months, maybe higher.

 Margin and Leverage: 

 Never go beyond 20% of margin. Keep the extra 30% up to 50% max for a rainy day black swan we get every 2 years. Rem if say one asset is your margin mattress and that asset drops 50%... your margin usage explodes. 

 Real Estate vs Stock: 

 For many, there is more money in stocks, skip real estate. 

 NVDA and Micron: 

 NVDA and Micron have the least risk of the AI plays. Crazy growth, huge demand, great value, and leader in the space.

 AI PE: 

 AI is still very cheap. MU is trading at a future PE of 8.

 ⁠Macro: 

 SF Rent:

 AI boom is impacting rental prices in SF increasing 22.6% YoY. Also sales prices of homes where many are selling in days and up to $1M over asking.

 BTC at $61500: 

 Buyers are stepping in each time now at $61,500.

 ETF Inflows: 

 ETF positives this week are green after 9 nasty red weeks.

 61 days Left:

 If the BTC cycle repeats, BTC bear market ends in only 61 days.

 Tesla and SPCX: 

 When will TESLA price go up? It all hinges on robotaxi scaling. IA feels it is coming soon, he sees all the signal in depots all around the nation. 

 Capital Allocator:

 Give your money to the best capital allocator on Earth and do not over think it. Look for those with amazing track records.
