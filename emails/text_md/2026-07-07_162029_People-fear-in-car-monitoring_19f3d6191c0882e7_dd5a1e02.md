 People are freaking out about in car monitoring law in Europe that went into effect today. They say it is Orwellian. They should not worry. Data is not shared. The camera data from the EU's new Advanced Driver Distraction Warning (ADDW) system is not reported to the government (or any third party). 

 In car camera monitoring is something that Tesla has done this for years.... required for FSD safety. This is a net positive for Tesla in Europe вҖ” it validates their hardware choices, limits disruption to their production, and gives them another competitive moat versus slower-moving rivals.
