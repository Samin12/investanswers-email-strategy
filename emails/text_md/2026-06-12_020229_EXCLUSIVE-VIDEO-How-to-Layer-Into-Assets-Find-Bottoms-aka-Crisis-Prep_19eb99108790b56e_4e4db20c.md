 Short TA Tips: Buying Dips + Crisis Prep 

 No Indicators 

 Zoom out to daily or weekly chart

 Buy near support (price floor that has held multiple times)

 Expect possible breakdown — have a plan

 One Simple Indicator 

 Use 200-day moving average

 Buying quality assets below it is generally a good long-term zone

 Helpful Add-Ons 

 ATR highlights clear support/resistance levels

 Optimized Trend shows if price is in an uptrend or just sideways

 How to Buy 

 Layer in: Start small on support, add more on deeper dips or higher lows

 Keep cash ready for panic moments

 Crisis Prep 

 Markets drop hard during fear — have dry powder to buy aggressively when others panic

 Don’t go all-in at once; scale in over time

 Bottom Line : Buy on support, layer in, stay liquid. You don’t need to catch the exact bottom — just good zones with room to add if it breaks lower.
