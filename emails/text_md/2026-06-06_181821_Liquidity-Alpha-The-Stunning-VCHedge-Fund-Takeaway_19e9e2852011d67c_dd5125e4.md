 I met w a lot of VCs and Hedge Funds

 My stunning takeaways from the conference: 1) most lack focus, they invest willy nilly across many sectors eg energy, biotech, tech, AI, agr, edu, you name it.... when u lack focus u lose. Focus is the edge. 

 2) the fees - see previous post

 3) talking to others about how frustrated they were w VCs and hedge funds and fees and poor returns. rem some are amazing... but most are not

 4) the lack of understanding around portfolio allocations... one guy who is always on CNBC had over 100 positions in his fund. Wut!!!! I thought 50 or 60 was ludicrous

 5) no use of risk mgt tools whatsoever, no options, no hedges, no covered calls etc

 Many of them have a God Complex but in reality... many of them I found to be complete doofuses (if that is a word). 

 NOTE RE RETURNS from Q above Rem when you DIVIDE these returns by M2 GROWTH you lost on them all.
