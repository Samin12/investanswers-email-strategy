 Tesla has electric infra all over the planet.... just shy of 80,000 - what they are is thousands of fully permitted, already-built power stations spread across the world. 

 Every AI company is hitting a wall trying to find enough electricity aka starved. 

 Enter the Megapod. By adding large battery storage systems (Megapacks) and solar at these sites, and a box full of GPUs - Tesla can soak up cheap off-peak power and deliver it exactly when AI systems need it most. It’s like they spent 14 years quietly building the gas stations of the AI era — and now those stations can flex to power both cars and the exploding demand for compute.

 The best part? This network is fully upgradable and ready to scale dramatically. What used to be “just chargers” is turning into one of the biggest moats in the entire AI race.

 It is almost too easy..... soon Tesla will be shitting Inference at Scale globally...
