 Retire On Model — we had a number of requests to increase the # of plan assets from 8 to 10 so we did that today. But always remember the best retirement plans aren’t built on hope. They’re built on clarity, discipline, and conviction. 

 The Conviction Rule: Fewer assets, higher conviction wins 

 The overwhelming majority of market gains come from a tiny handful of winners....roughly the top 0.3%. Unless you’re lucky enough to own all of them (spoiler: you won’t), every extra asset you add waters down your returns. High-conviction investors don’t try to own everything. They own the best things. The ones they understand deeply and believe in strongly.

 Although we increased Retire On to 10 assets per plan, we’re still telling you the same thing we’ve always said: use fewer, not more. Focus beats diversification every single time when it comes to building wealth.

 And also keep in mind that a well-defined plan is your best defense. Retire On shows you exactly what you need, when you need it, and how you can spend it (without guessing or hoping). It's a disciplined, no-BS roadmap to personal financial freedom.
