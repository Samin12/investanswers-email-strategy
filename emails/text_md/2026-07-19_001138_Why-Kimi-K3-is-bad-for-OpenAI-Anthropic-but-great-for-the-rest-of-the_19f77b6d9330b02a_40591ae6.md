 Kimi K3 may be an important inflection point for AI. Potentially negative for Anthropic and OpenAI while being net positive for essentially every other company in the world.

 Why? Kimi K3 is bullish for NVIDIA primarily because its massive 2.8 trillion parameter size makes it extremely hardware-demanding for efficient large-scale inference — and NVIDIA's latest rack-scale systems (especially the GB200 NVL72) are uniquely positioned to handle it well

 Kimi K3 isn't just another model — its size forces buyers toward premium, NVIDIA-optimized infrastructure. That's a direct tailwind for NVDA's data center revenue in the ongoing AI buildout aka most of the IA13.
