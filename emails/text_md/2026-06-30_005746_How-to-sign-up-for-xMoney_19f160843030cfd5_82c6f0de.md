 X Money was first for select users in the US (often starting with X Premium or Premium+ accounts). Not sure when Europe will follow... given regulations perhaps never. 

 How to sign up for X Money

 Update the X app to the latest version (very important — older versions won’t show the feature).

 Open the X app and log in.

 Tap your profile picture (top left) to open the side menu.

 Look for Money, Payments, or a wallet icon ($) — it may also appear in the bottom navigation bar.

 If you see it, tap it and follow the on-screen prompts to enroll.

 The signup process is quick:

 Enter your name, address, and last 4 digits of your SSN.

 Verify with Face ID, PIN, or biometrics.

 Complete identity verification (KYC).

 Once approved, you can send/receive money to other X users, get a virtual/physical X Card, earn interest on balances, and more.Eligibility (as of June 2026):

 U.S. resident, 18+

 Verified U.S. phone number

 X account in good standing

 Currently rolling out in phases (not everyone sees the tab yet)
