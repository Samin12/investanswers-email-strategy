 Photonics on the other hand is sending data at teh speed of light. Photonics has become the new critical bottleneck after the chips themselves. Why our IA13 names like ALAB AVGO and MRVL have done well.... 

 Re AAOI while it has also been on fire the financials are not as solid as the others. 

 Re LITE appears to be diluting and very overvalued.
