 Anthropic is reportedly preparing a new funding round that could value the company at nearly $1 trillion, propelled by an extraordinary surge in revenue and enterprise adoption.

 Its Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) exploded from $1 billion in January 2025 to $30 billion by April 2026 — a 30× increase in just over a year. Current projections suggest Anthropic could surpass Alphabet in revenue by mid-2028 as it shifts from selling software tools to directly monetizing “cognitive capacity” in the global labor market.

 Yet this is AI in 2026 — and things move at a blistering pace. Some are saying, like David Sacks Anthropic could become the biggest company on the planet. 

 HOWEVER... I expect SpaceXAI to reach parity with, and quite possibly exceed, Claude’s capabilities within the next 6–12 months. Esp w Cursor and Macrohard. When that happens, the competitive landscape (and these revenue trajectories) could shift dramatically. The companies that look unassailable today can find themselves in a very different position tomorrow.
