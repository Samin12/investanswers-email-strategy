 Microsoft is reportedly testing China’s Moonshot AI Kimi K3 for Copilot as it adds the open-weight model to Azure. Engineers will evaluate whether K3 can power features currently running on OpenAI and Anthropic models after it topped Arena’s coding leaderboard and overwhelmed Moonshot’s GPU capacity with demand.

 The move is driven by Microsoft’s push for higher margins — especially as both OpenAI and Anthropic are now selling their models directly through Codex and Claude. OpenAI and Anthropic models are also expensive to run.
