 Jensen mentioned his 5 layer cake last week. I was one to notice the Capex Flows Early - hence the IA13.... and Jordy came out w a nice phrase "Your CapEx Is My Opportunity". 

 For the last 20+ years, the winning investment idea was "Your margin is my opportunity" — software companies (like the big tech giants) used cheap code to steal profits from traditional businesses (think Jeff Bezos and Amazon).

 That era is over. The new winning idea is "Your CapEx is my opportunity."

 AI has moved from just being clever software into a massive physical buildout. It now needs enormous real-world infrastructure: power plants, data centers, chips, cooling systems, electrical grids, factories, and raw materials. This is the "$100 trillion physical world upgrade."

 But the real money is now flowing to the companies that build and supply the physical stuff AI needs.

 The Five Layers of the AI Cake Top layer — Apps and AI agents (what most people see)

 Models — The brains (Grok, Gemini, Claude etc.)

 Data - aka the fuel

 Chips, computers, and networks

 Power — Energy, factories, raw materials, and physical hardware ← This is where the biggest shortages and opportunities are right now
