 Wow the S1 changed everything for me. Great Q from Dan D

 Here is what is new and changes my investing calculus since yesterday. 

 Core revenue came in stronger than expected with real profitability 2025 revenue hit $18.67 billion (higher than many private estimates of ~$15-16B). 

 The Anthropic AI compute deal is a game-changer and bigger than many expected SpaceX has a $1.25 billion per month (~$15 billion per year) cloud services agreement with Anthropic through May 2029 for AI compute on their Colossus and Colossus II systems. I thought it was $8BN. Then came Elon’s tweet 2 hrs ago about doing it w more players (and being in discussions with others) now has a concrete, massive anchor. It proves they can monetize the huge capex they’re spending on GPUs/supercomputers.

 Bitcoin treasury is larger/more valuable than anticipated SpaceX holds 18,712 BTC

 The long-term AI + orbital data center story just got a lot more credible The S-1 frames a $28.5 trillion total addressable market (excluding China/Russia), with $26.5 trillion tied to AI (infrastructure, applications, consumer, advertising). No longer sci fi. 

 The S1 de-risked the vision SpaceX - makes the “we’ll dominate space-based AI compute” thesis feel more grounded than pure speculation.
