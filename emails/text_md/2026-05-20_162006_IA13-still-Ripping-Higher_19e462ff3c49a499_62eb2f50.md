 ALAB post yesterday combined with broad strength across the AI /semiconductor complex and NVDA earnings later today are making this AI world pop. Astera Labs had a massive analyst upgrade yesterday (May 19) from Evercore ISI, which raised the price target sharply to $297 (from $215) while keeping an Outperform rating. 

 ARM (+15%): Benefiting from the same AI/data center tailwinds. 

 AMD (+7.9%): Part of the ongoing AI accelerator and CPU strength. Recent positive commentary/outlook on AI demand has helped lift the sector. 

 ASML (+6.1%): Strong recent results and continued bullish commentary on AI-driven chip demand and equipment orders. 

 MRVL (+5.7%): AI networking and custom silicon exposure. 

 MU, TSM, AVGO, NVDA: All participating in the broader AI/memory/foundry/accelerator rally.

 Tesla moving also.
