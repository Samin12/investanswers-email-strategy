 Chart shows China's growing ability to produce its own AI hardware, specifically Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) and rely less on importing them. 

 Today, China is supplying about 41% of its own GPU needs domestically. China should be over 85% self-sufficient by the year 2030.

 Being what will prob be the biggest consumer of Chips this is a big deal.
