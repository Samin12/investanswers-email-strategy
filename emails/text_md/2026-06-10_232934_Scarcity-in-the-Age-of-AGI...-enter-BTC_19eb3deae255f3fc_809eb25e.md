 Ive been saying for a while - in the age of AGI scarcity will become very valuable... AI agents will not fuel bitcoin, but wealth from AI will. 

 Sophisticated investors and high-net-worth individuals, after generating monster returns in technology and AI shares, often allocate capital toward scarce, high-quality assets that have historically served as long-term stores of value. As a result, there is a meaningful possibility that a portion of the capital unlocked through the wave of major AI and technology IPOs — representing trillions of dollars in potential market value — could ultimately flow into Bitcoin, particularly if it remains attractively valued relative to other opportunities.
