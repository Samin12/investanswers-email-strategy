 TLDR Tesla is my HUMANOID PLAY

 The Details Neura Robotics is one of Germany’s hottest private robotics companies. Founded in 2019, it has already grown to over $100 million in annual revenue while staying independent.

 Quick Growth Story Started in 2019 by David Reger.

 Raised $55 million in 2023 to expand and build more robots.

 Raised another €120 million ($123 million) in early 2025 — one of the biggest funding rounds for a European company.

 Today it’s a profitable private company doing $100M+ in revenue.

 Key Products & Numbers LARA (launched 2020): A helpful robot arm used in factories, hospitals, and warehouses.

 MAV: A mobile robot that carries up to 1.5 tons (like a small car) and works alongside people without stopping production.

 4NE1 (launched 2024): Human-like robot, 1.8 meters tall (about 6 feet), weighs 80 kg, can carry 15 kg, and moves up to 3 km/h. Built for both factory work and home use.

 Big Partnerships Works with Kawasaki to build collaborative robots.

 Joined Jensen's Nvidia’s humanoid robot program in 2024 to speed up development.

 Partnered with Bosch in January 2026 to scale human-like robots for factories.

 Part of a major industry group working on standard robot connections for factories.

 In just 7 years, this private German company went from startup to $100M+ revenue with real products shipping and major global partners. It’s one of the fastest-rising robotics players in Europe.
