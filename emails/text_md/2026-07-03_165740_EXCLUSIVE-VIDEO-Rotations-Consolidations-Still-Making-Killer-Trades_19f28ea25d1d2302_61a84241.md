 Key Technical Analysis "Tricks" Optimized Trend color flip: blue = bullish/uptrend bias, orange = bearish/downtrend bias

 50-Day Moving Average (Rising 50-Day = Short-Term Strength) 

 200-Day Moving Average (Major Battleground Support) 

 Major support zones for NVDA, AVGO, TSLA (~368), Google.

 ATR Levels (Volatility-Based Support/Resistance Bands — Levels 1–6) 

 Level 4 and Level 5 frequently highlighted as high-probability stacking/accumulation zones during pullbacks.

 Strong stocks often base between Level 5–6 before breaking higher.

 Used across MRVL, ARM, PLTR, ALAB, GOOGL, ASML, Crypto.

 Price Action Reversals + Support/Resistance Flip 

 Reclaiming a prior resistance level as new support = bullish.

 Example: PLTR reclaiming the ATR Level 4 area.

 Psychological / Whole Number Levels 

 PLTR at $100 as obvious support.

 Deviation Analysis (Correlated Assets) 

 MSTR broke down harder than BTC → reversal it goes up far faster 

 "Load the Boat" / Accumulate on Dips in High-Conviction Names
