 As you know all my life, I've been obsessed with total addressable markets. Step one of investing is pick a leader in the space that is addressing a very large or even the largest total addressable market(s). That's my first step and I've just mapped out the TAM of Tesla across my new 20 different lines of business. This will be a key part of my NEW valuation model for Tesla. 

 Net net - Tesla is tackling a $76 Trillion TAM

 For perspective, SpaceX is going after $28.5 Trillion TAM - also huge but a third of what tesla is going after.
