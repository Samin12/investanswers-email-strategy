 Lot's of bubble talk and fear is up to 100 out there... wanted to share a few short notes

 Almost nobody is using it yet Big companies are less than 1% into using real AI. Regular workers are at basically 0%. We’re still at the very beginning.

 The best AI just learned how to code by itself In 2025 the tools went from “helps a little” to today “can build whole projects on its own.” Even non-coders can now use it. This one change alone could be worth TRILLIONS

 We’re already running out of compute - there isn’t enough chip power, data centers, or electricity power right now — and demand is exploding. 

 REM You can’t have a bubble when supply can’t keep up. 

 The top AI companies are pulling away 

 The “picks and shovels” are making HUGE money. 

 The real money comes from Baby AGI and AGI... think self driving cars, humanoids etc... it bears repeating...
