 The AI Infra Bet is simple: AI needs massive compute, compute needs cheap power, and space is the cheapest place to get it.

 Everyone else is renting floors in someone else's building.

 While Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic each own just one piece (models, chips, or data centers) and rely on others for the rest, SpaceX controls or soon will control everything:

 Its own frontier AI model (Grok)

 Real-time training data from X

 Custom chips it designs and makes

 Its own rockets to launch stuff

 Starlink to deliver it anywhere on Earth

 And now orbital data centers in space, powered by free solar energy with basically free cooling
