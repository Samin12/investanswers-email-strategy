 Per my message yesterday re those who felt they missed the train, they did not. We are still super early in AI. The best part of this story is only just beginning. As Goldman. 

 Goldman Sachs report was good. Agentic AI (smart AI agents that can think, use tools, and work on their own) is about to flip the economics of AI.

 Right now, AI feels like a massive money pit with huge upfront costs, Capex etc. But as the cost of running AI keeps dropping, these smarter agents become affordable. And here’s the key: these agents don’t just use a little AI — they use a lot of it. They think longer, check their work, use tools, and run in the background 24/7.

 This means way more usage, which actually makes the business more profitable over time. Data centers get used better, profits per dollar improve, and companies can reinvest that money into making AI even stronger.

 The real bull case for AI isn’t just “more people will use it.” It’s that usage growth will start driving strong profits starting in 2026.
