 Some guy buys $4 million worth of BONK tokens. Then he posts a proposal in their DAO that basically says: “Hey, can the DAO just send me $20 million in BONK?”

 He waits seven full days . Nobody checks the forum. Nobody pushes back. Crickets.

 When it’s time to vote, he votes yes using the BONK he just bought. Since he now owns a big enough chunk, his vote is enough to pass the proposal.

 Just like that, $20 million gets transferred straight into his wallet.

 The BONK team only realized what happened after the money was already gone.

 This wasn’t even a hack. There was no exploit, no code vulnerability, nothing technical. The proposal just passed because almost nobody was paying attention to the governance forum.

 It’s the crypto version of leaving the vault door wide open, writing “take whatever you want” on a whiteboard, and then being shocked when someone actually does.

 DAOs are DUMB
