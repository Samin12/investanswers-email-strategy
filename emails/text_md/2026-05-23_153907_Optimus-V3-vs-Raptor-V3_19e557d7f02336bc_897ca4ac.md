 Why SpaceX can show Raptor V3 and NOT Optimus V3 

 I have read and watched everything I can on both. When SpaceX shows Raptor 3 firing, we only see the outside of the engine. The real secret sauce is hidden inside: the special metals, tiny cooling channels, precision plumbing, advanced manufacturing, and years of painful trial and error. Elon even spoke about moving Atoms to make it. 

 Even with every camera angle, competitors still cannot see how the materials are made, how the internals are built, or how to make the engine reliable. You cannot just copy the shape and expect it to work. Rocket engines are insanely hard to manufacture.

 Why Optimus is different 

 Optimus is much easier to copy from the outside.

 With a humanoid robot, the body shape, joint placement, arm and hand movement, walking style, and overall design reveal a lot of the engineering. Detailed videos can give competitors big clues

 Chinese manufacturers are EXCELLENT at copying, at watching, measuring, cloning, sourcing parts, and iterating fast. Even if Tesla hides the best electronics and actuators inside, the public design still reveals enough for others to build similar-looking robots much faster than people expect.
