 First out of the gate, Bill Ackman says stock markets are going way higher!!!! He does not see a bubble. In fact he believes many names are actually very cheap given the AI revolution. 

 Bill Ackman shared his 4-Point Framework for assessing every single investment Ackman boils it down to four key things you should always look at:

 People — This is one of the most important factors. Who’s running the show? Do they have the talent, drive, and track record to execute? I have always done this subconciously. 

 Opportunity — Also one of the biggest things. How large is the market or problem they’re going after? Is it a small niche or something massive that can grow for decades?

 Context — The bigger picture. What’s happening in the world right now (technology shifts, competition, regulation, timing)? This matters a lot.

 Deal — This one is the hardest and most complex. What are you actually paying? What are the terms, what is timing, risks, and potential dilution? Getting the price and structure right takes real work. Eg to buy or not to buy the SpaceX IPO.
