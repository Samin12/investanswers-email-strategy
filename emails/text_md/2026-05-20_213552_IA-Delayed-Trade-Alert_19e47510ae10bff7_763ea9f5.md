 On the original trade: the puts I sold to establish my ALAB position — the loss I took closing them equates to roughly half the gains I originally made selling those puts. So yes, I left some money on the table. But it’s nowhere near the gains I’ve already locked in from selling the puts, and especially not compared to the LEAPS, which have exploded in value.

 It is imperative for me to keep this position as I did not want to be called out and I still have more to buy back....
