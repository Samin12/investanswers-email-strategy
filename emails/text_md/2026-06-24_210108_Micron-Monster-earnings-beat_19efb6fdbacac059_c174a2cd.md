 MU up $150 in after hours - over 14% 

 For fiscal Q3 2026, Micron reported:

 Revenue : $41.46 billion (Wall Street expected $35.5B) → +17% beat 

 Adjusted EPS : $25.11 (expected $20.40) → big beat 

 Gross Margin : 84.9% (expected 81.8%) — extremely high

 The biggest surprise came from AI-related segments. Core Data Center revenue hit $11.52 billion vs $6.8 billion expected — a massive ~70% beat. Cloud Memory also crushed it.

 Even Stronger Guidance Micron guided Q4 revenue to $50 billion (±$1B) — well above the $43.4B analysts were modeling — with EPS around $31 and gross margins rising to 86% .

 Micron is seeing explosive demand for high-end memory (especially HBM) that powers AI training and inference. They're already shipping HBM4 in high volume, and they expect tight supply conditions to continue well beyond 2027 because AI demand is so strong across data centers, cloud, and other areas.

 MU is also dragging up all our other AI names.
