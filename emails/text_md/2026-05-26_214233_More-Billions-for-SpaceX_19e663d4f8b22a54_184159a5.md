 U.S. Space Force awards SpaceX $2.29B contract for Starshield’s Space Data Network (SDN) Backbone.

 This is a next-generation secure military satellite communications network in low Earth orbit, separate from commercial Starlink. Starshield uses enhanced encryption and security protocols to handle classified data and payloads.

 The system will serve as a high-speed, low-latency global data transport layer, connecting military sensors, weapons systems, satellites, aircraft, drones, and missile defenses through a resilient mesh constellation with optical inter-satellite links.

 A fully operational prototype is required by the end of 2027 under the OTA agreement.
