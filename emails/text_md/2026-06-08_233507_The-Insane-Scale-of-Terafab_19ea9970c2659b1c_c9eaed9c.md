 SpaceX with Tesla and xAI is building Terafab, Biggest Chip Factory Ever. It is 100 million square feet. That’s 10 times larger than Tesla’s already huge Giga Texas factory.

 Output goal: 1 terawatt (TW) of chips per year

 For comparison: The entire United States uses about 0.5 TW of electricity on average. So this one factory aims to produce chips every year with roughly twice the power capacity of the whole U.S. grid.

 It will make advanced chips (for AI, memory, and packaging) all in one giant building.

 Why this scale is insane: The chips are needed for two huge things:

 Hundreds of gigawatts worth of chips just for Tesla’s Optimus humanoid robots.

 Terawatts more for SpaceX’s planned solar-powered AI satellites in orbit (space-based data centers that get constant sunlight and free cooling).

 To build all this, they’ll eventually need to launch millions of tons of equipment into space w starship every year — and they plan to use up to 1 billion Optimus robots to help construct and run everything.

 This is why I am bullish on Muskonomy - nobdoy has ever even attempted a fraction of this scale nevermind all at once across so many fields... chips, energy, space, humanoids etc.... it is WILD
