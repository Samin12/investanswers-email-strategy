 Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.8 - it's positioned as more reliable for real production work like code reviews, audits, and complex analysis.

 The big "news" everyone is talking about now online is Zcash bug discovery.Thought it was fake news at the beginning but as it turns out it is not. 

 Claude Opus 4.8 was used to audit Zcash's code. It uncovered a critical vulnerability in the Orchard shielded pool (Zcash's privacy tech, live since 2022). The bug could have let attackers mint unlimited counterfeit ZEC inside the shielded pool—essentially an "infinite money glitch" that would have been undetectable due to Zcash's strong privacy features. It stemmed from insufficient verification in the circuit.

 What actually happened:

 Zcash devs responded fast.

 Soft fork on June 2 disabled Orchard transactions.

 Hard fork (NU6.2) on the same day patched it.

 NOTE I AM NOT SAYING ZEC IS DEAD BUT I AM HIGHLIGHTING THE RISKS and for the record I never bought into ZEC despite lots of pressure.
