 This is my situation. 

 My avg on STRC is about 95 to 96... i bagged 9 mths of 1%.... my breakeven is approx $86.5 so I am underwater as price is now once again below $83. Taking a 3.5% or 4% loss is not a hardship. But lets dig deeper. 

 CEO bought $1M but the market does not care. 

 Last Thursday buyers stepped in at this level... not sure if they will today as BTC is so weak. 

 Blackrock BTC ETF is still dumping. This is dragging BTC down. STRC is 93% correlated to BTC> 

 THE MATH OF WHERE WE ARE Probability of STRC going to zero (or near-zero): Extremely unlikely in any reasonable timeframe. They have 11 mths of dividend in cash. Probability estimate: <2–5% over the next 2–3 years (closer to 1–3% tail risk).

 The options: Hold for the yield while monitoring

 Buy a put - but they are v expensive. 

 Next dividend rate adjustment and moving to two week dividends could help. 

 Other option is trim a portion to derisk.
