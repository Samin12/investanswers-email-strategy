 A millionaire in 1903 was rich. A millionaire in the future is a down payment and a nervous retirement. 

 The chart shows Bitcoin's price rising dramatically over decades вҖ” but it's also illustrating how much the US dollar keeps losing value (debasement) over time.

 The "number go up" on Bitcoin isn't happening in a vacuum. Part of why BTC's USD price climbs so high is precisely because the unit it's measured ie the dollar is steadily losing value. You're watching two curves move in opposite directions on the real-wealth scale.

 So Bitcoin be worth $180 million in 2075, but you're not watching Bitcoin climb to 180 million. You're watching the dollar fall until 180 million is the new million.
