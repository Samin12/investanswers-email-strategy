 Pantera have a new crypto index. It is designed as a fundamentals-driven, rules-based benchmark for institutional investors, focusing on digital assets that demonstrate real economic activity rather than pure speculation, market hype, or simple market-cap weighting. 

 Key Holdings Ether (ETH), Solana (SOL), Binance Coin (BNB), Tron (TRX), and Hyperliquid (HYPE). They have a new revenue requirement that heavily favors smart contract platforms and DeFi protocols. Note this index does not hold any Bitcoin or XRP. 

 The rules for inclusion Protocols must show positive protocol-level revenue over the two most recent quarters 

 Revenue is verified via on-chain data (powered by Artemis).

 The revenue must accrue to token holders in meaningful ways (e.g., token burns, staking yields net of inflation, buybacks, distributions, or tokenholder-controlled treasuries).
