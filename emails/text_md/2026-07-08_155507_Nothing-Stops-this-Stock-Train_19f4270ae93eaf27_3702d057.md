 Nothing Stops This Train 

 Eric Balchunas a top ETF analyst shared a note above... and why it is profound. 

 The US stock market isn't just for rich people anymore вЂ” it's basically become America's PENSION. A huge chunk of regular people's savings now sits in stocks.

 Over half of all Americans (55%) already own stocks вЂ” more than any other country in the world. That's a massive number of voters who care what happens to the market.

 New "Trump Kids Accounts" are expected to pull in another 28 million Americans. Soon, most people (rich, middle class, and lower income) will have real money riding on the stock market.

 Follow The Money When that many voters have skin in the game, politicians will feel huge pressure to protect the market from long, painful drops. They won't want angry retirees and workers showing up at the polls. Because of this, the Federal Reserve will probably start buying stock funds (like ETFs) during the next big crash to keep the market from falling too far. China and Japan already do this kind of thing. They may even target specific industries or big companies.

 Most Perma Bear Experts still miss this completely. That's why the "the market is going to crash hard" crowd keeps getting financiall ruined eg Michael Burry, Jim Chanus, etc. Regular investors already seem to understand it though вЂ” they've been buying stock funds even during dips.

 Another insane stat A survey of 1,000 people showed that 3 out of 4 now expect the government to bail out the stock market in the next crisis.
