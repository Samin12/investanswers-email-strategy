 ASML, the leader in lithography, just said today that its plans to increase production in 2027 and 2028 already include the machines needed for Elon Musk’s planned Terafab chip factory in Texas.

 The company’s CFO, Roger Dassen, explained that ASML regularly talks with all its major customers about their future factory plans. He confirmed that Terafab is one of the major projects they’re factoring in.

 Dassen also said ASML is nearly sold out of its most advanced machines for 2027 and has already secured a large number of orders for those same machines in 2028.
