 The company has not even IPOd yet and there are already at least 21 ETPs filed by 12 different issuers. Insane - $SPCX demand will be interesting to watch. 

 PS Jeff Bezos Blue Origin blew up last nite. It has some implications for Space Stocks. 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This was supposed to be New Glenn’s first really heavy payload — 48 satellites (~30 tons). Amazon is racing to build its space internet constellation to compete with Starlink and must meet deadlines to activate service and keep its government radio frequencies. With limited backup options (Falcon 9 has east-coast/barge constraints, Vulcan is struggling, Ariane 6 is slow), this creates a real bottleneck for Amazon’s timeline. I personally dont see Bezos making another Starlink... he is toooo far behind. 

 AST Space Mobile: Also relying on New Glenn for big satellite launches. They now face the same capacity crunch. Also toast. ASTS down 18% today. 

 ULA / Vulcan rocket uses Blue Origin’s BE-4 engines. Even though the engines may not be at fault, no idea as there is no evidence left, expect more delays for ULA.

 NASA is also counting on Blue Origin’s Blue Moon lunar lander for the Artemis program - no idea why NASA are not doing it all themselves rather than outsource it as they did it many times already 60 years ago w less than than an iPhone. 

 Why This Is Good for SpaceX - what I see so far More business flows to SpaceX - everyone else struggling. 

 No other heavy lift capability.
