 The MiCA transitional/grandfathering period ends on July 1, 2026 across the EU/EEA. After this date, any crypto-asset service provider (CASP) without full MiCA authorization must stop serving EU users or face enforcement. There are no extensions. 

 As a result 92% of Crypto firms based in Europe will shut down tomorrow. Only 244 out of 3000 firms secured a MiCA license.
