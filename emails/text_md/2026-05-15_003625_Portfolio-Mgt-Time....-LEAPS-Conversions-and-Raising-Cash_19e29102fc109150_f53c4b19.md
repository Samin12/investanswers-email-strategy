 Q came in from Winners above. The answer is always simple... I buy LEAPS on companies I want to own long term and the lower they go the harder I go. I always convert unless thesis changes. 

 So this Q gives me the chance to update you all on my cash crunch right now. I’m a big believer in buying LEAPS — let me control a lot of shares for a fraction of the price. I only do this on companies I actually want to own for years. The lower the stock drops, the more aggressively I buy these LEAPS. REM WHEN U CONVERT A LEAP THERE IS NO CAPITAL GAINS TAX.. you just get to buy the stock cheap... avoiding the TAX DRAG. 

 When Tesla crashed down to $106 , I went pretty crazy. I loaded up on cheap out-of-the-money LEAPS (the 140s, 150s, 160s, and 175s). A bunch of them expire in December 2025, and I’ve got some shorter ones coming due next month in June.

 Here’s why this matters for cash: Right now Tesla is around $450 . If I want to turn those LEAPS into actual Tesla shares, I have to pay the strike price. So for example:

 To get 100 shares of Tesla, I only have to pay $14,000 right now… but those 100 shares are worth $45,000 .

 For 10 contracts that’s $450,000 - I need to come up with $140K.

 For 100 contracts it’s $4.5 million - I need to come up with $1.4M. etc etc

 That’s the big bill that’s about to hit me.

 Back in April I got crushed by taxes on my capital gains, so I basically blew through all my cash. I even had to borrow money on margin just so I wouldn’t have to touch my STRC account.

 Now I’m in another cash squeeze. To raise money I’ve been:

 Selling covered calls like crazy on Google, AMD, ALAB, STM, etc.

 Shorting oil - killer trade

 Shorting Avis - killer trade

 Those moves have actually worked out pretty well, but they’re riskier than I would normally do and I don’t love doing them at this scale.

 The biggest pressure right now is that big Tesla LEAP conversion coming up. It’s forcing me to sell even more calls and exit other positions to free up cash.

 THE LEAPS COMING DUE in 2026 and i know many of you have them also... CONVERT - this thing is going to $2000 easy. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 What I’m doing about it: I’m letting my STM shares get called away at $55 (I don’t mind — I got them cheap in an SL a long time ago).

 I might have to sell my STRC position

 I’m working on one more strategy to raise cash… think dumping big assets - I’ll let you know when it’s ready.

 Bottom line: I’m not broke, but I’m temporarily cash-poor because I went really heavy on these long-term Tesla bets. The good news is the Tesla position is worth a fortune on paper — I just need to find the cash to exercise the options and turn them into real shares without selling everything at a bad time.
