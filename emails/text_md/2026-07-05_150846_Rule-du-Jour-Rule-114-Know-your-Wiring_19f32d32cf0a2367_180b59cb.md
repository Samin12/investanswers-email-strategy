 Know your wiring and engineer around weakness. Take the free Investor Profiler above if you have not already. 

 Cool new study re Bitcoiners digs into Personality Types, something I have been obsessed with since the 1990s, knowing how your brain is wired, knowing your weaknesses is the FIRST STEP in becoming a brilliant investor. 

 Now this takes me to Bitcoiners: 90% of Bitcoiners are Intuitive (N) types vs. ~27% of the general population.

 ~71-72% are Analyst types (INTJ, INTP, ENTJ, ENTP) — nearly 7x overrepresented.

 INTJ : 2.1% of general pop → 34.7% of Bitcoiners ( 17x overrepresentation).

 INTP : 3.3% of general pop → 22.6% of Bitcoiners.

 In my early studies from decades ago it was clear INTJs had an edge but was not sure why so hence we studied it for years.... Now that being said, even the strongest INTJ profile has predictable blind spots that can destroy returns: 

 Overconfidence in the model → slow to update when new evidence appears

 Difficulty cutting losers or re-evaluating thesis when fundamentals shift

 Often bad at selling tops

 Under-appreciating liquidity needs or behavioral market dynamics

 Perfectionism that causes missed entries or oversized positions once conviction hits

 Sometimes weak on process/risk management because “the thesis is sound”

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This is exactly why the FREE InvestAnswers Investor Profiler is so valuable. It doesn’t replace MBTI insights — it complements them with a practical, investor-specific lens (Opportunist vs. Cautious Planner vs. hybrid). It forces you to confront your specific biases around risk, position sizing, FOMO, conservatism, etc., so you can build rules and systems that protect you from yourself.

 The best investors aren’t the ones with the “perfect” personality. They’re the ones who know their wiring and deliberately engineer around its weaknesses in order to get killer results.
