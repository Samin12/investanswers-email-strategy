 I asked Grok what best performers would. be over the next 30 years. The AI answer gave me some comfort given my allocations. 

 The Joint Winners "Bitcoin edges out as the single option with the highest probability of creating the most wealth over 30 years for a risk-tolerant investor, thanks to its scarcity, adoption momentum, and asymmetric payoff profile. 

 Tesla and SpaceX were super close behind (basically even) because they are actively building transformative technologies in AI, robotics, energy, and space—the very forces that could reshape economies."

 Summary The "most wealth" will likely flow to assets tied to scarcity + exponential technological progress (Bitcoin as hard money + the companies enabling AI/robotics/space abundance).
