 I found this interesting, back in the day (1990s) most wealth was in the home.... today it has flipped. 

 The average household has roughly $357k–$384k in home value (gross, before mortgage). After subtracting the mortgage portion, home equity is lower — roughly $250k on average.

 The average household now has ~$506k in directly and indirectly held equities ie the 46% bucket. Good to see as Stocks will make you far richer over time if you pick the right ones. 

 Note these are avg figures — ie they are skewed upward by wealthy households.
